OVER VIEW

Bachelor of Unani Medicine and Surgery (BUMS) is an undergraduate medical course. Bachelor of Unani medicine and surgery is a course that is coming under the Medical Field. Unani medicine is a traditional method of cure which is a form of traditional medicine practiced in Middle- East and South-Asian countries. Students who are interested in learning about Unani Medicine can pursue this course.
Unani Medicine and Surgery is a comprehensive system of medicine. It is not only the original science of medicine but also a rich storehouse of principles and philosophies of medicine that can be of immense value to medicine in particular and science in general. Unani Medicine is an alternative traditional medical system, which deals with the various states of health and disease. It provides promotive, preventive, curative, and rehabilitative healthcare.

Educational Requirements and Admissions

The admission required for the entrance of Bachelor of Unani Medicine and Surgery (BUMS) is the successful completion of (10+2) or equivalent science subjects (Physics, Chemistry, and Biology with 50% marks)with an optional subject as Urdu, Arabic or Persian at High school level. Candidates should attend the age of 17 to appear for the examination.
Admission for the BUMS course is based on the score obtained by the student in 10+2 and the score obtained in the exams conducted by the national or state examination authorities. A candidate who has passed the Pre-Tib examination of one-year duration is also eligible for admission to Bachelor of Unani Medicine and Surgery (BUMS).
