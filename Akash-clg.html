OVERVIEW

Akash Institute of Medical Sciences and Research Centre(AIMS&RC), Bangalore was Founded in 2014, this college has quickly become one of the most popular and leading medical colleges in Bangalore. A blend of human connections and values with cultural heritage is the main concept that encompases the value of higher education that Akash Institute of Medical Sciences and Research Centre provides. This college provides all the facilities and means to ensure the best medical education in the country. This institute runs on the principle of positive interpersonal skills and equality regardless of race, community or caste. Access to global standards of education with the best of facilities and environments for self study is the main goal in Akash Institute of Medical Sciences and Research Centre (AIMSRC).Established under Akash Education & Development Trust, the institute provides students with top notch quality education, research facilities and health services to members of the society at affordable costs. AIMS&RC's mission is to aspire for worldwide excellence in medical education and patient care. The commitment to building an institute where professionalism, competence, and compassion are the key principles instilled in doctors who emerge from this institute.

Education at Akash Institute of Medical Sciences and Research Centre

Education at this college provides various facilities and scope to become the best in your profession. You can get Higher education in Medical Sciences with amenities like Placement assistance cell, Seminars, workshops, industry level, Central library, Wifi enabled and Well established labs. With these top notch facilities, this is a dream college for any medical aspirant. The college provides various opportunities for student research activity which helps them in learning through discovery.For better exposure to clinical sides, students are taken to frequent hospital visits which give them an insight into how the professional world functions, thus motivating them to excel not only theoretically by cramming their textbooks but also practically as a professional doctor.

ADMISSION

The College offers Graduation and Post – Graduation courses such as MBBS, MD, and Diploma courses in Anatomy, Physiology, Microbiology, Pediatrics, Gastronomy and many other branches. The admission is given on the basis of NEET and other All India Examinations for various courses like BPT, BDS, Nursing and other courses. Some of the courses also require the candidate to appear for personal interview rounds. The entrance examinations are usually held around the month of May and June.

CAMPUS

Akash Institute of Medical Sciences and Research Centre, a brand new campus stretching over several acres, is located in Bangalore city, right near Kempegowda International Airport, and has a huge campus with an abundance of amenities and infrastructure. This college's campus is vibrant and joyful, with a strong growth environment. One of the best places to study and learn, with excellent materials that are easily available. A fully functional Affiliated teaching hospital is also located immediately close to the college campus. The hospital's infrastructure and facilities is in accordance with the terms and guidance provided by Medical Council of India (MCI) There are several blocks, including an administrative block, an inpatient block, an outpatient block, wards, a critical care unit, a casualty unit, and an emergency ward. There are distinct facilities and wards for males, women, and physically disabled patients. The hospital has a nurses duty room and a demonstration room for educational reasons. There is also a treatment and examination room, department offices, and teaching staff quarters.

INFRASTRUCTURE

ADVANCED LEARNING CENTER:

The Advanced Learning Center (ALC) has been established to provide hands-on training and organize workshops where students are taught basic and advanced surgical skills as well as teach specific methods of surgeries within orthopedics, ENT, invasive procedures, neurosurgery, general surgery It is always accessible by students, faculty and the nursing staff. It also organizes frequent workshops and implant validation programs.

OPERATION LABS:

There are two types of labs in the College.

  1. Medical Skills Lab- Here the students are taught the practical basics of surgery, life support, advanced cardiac life support, and other skills at post-graduate, graduate, and nursing levels using mannequins, models, and simulation techniques.
  2. Cadaveric Research and Training Unit– Here the students get to opportunity to have specific training to hone their practical skills

FAQ

How can I join Akash Medical Institute?

The process of joining Akash institute of Medical Sciences and Research Centre is straightforward. Medical aspirants must undertake the NEET examination, based on their rank and score, they would be eligible for common counselling where based on the seats available, they can choose amongst the colleges they can opt in the ranking range. This is the process to apply and join any government or Private medical college in India.

What is the fee of Aakash Institute for Medical?

The fees in this college ranges from 55.98 LAKHS for MBBS to 5.7 LAKHS for MD in Common Medicine or Pharmacology.

When did Akash medical college start?

Established under Akash Education & Development Trust, Akash Institute of Medical Sciences and Research Centre(AIMS&RC) was Founded in 2014 in Bangalore, Karnataka.
