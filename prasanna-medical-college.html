OVER VIEW

Prasanna College of Ayurveda and hospital was established in January 2013 under the supervision of Prasanna education trust, Belthangady, D.K Karnataka. The college is affiliated with Rajiv Gandhi institute of health and science, Bangalore, and is approved by the central council of Indian medicine.

The college was established since the ex-minister of govt of Karnataka wanted to provide better and effective healthcare facilities to the people who live in the nearby surrounding. The trust also provides education to students in different disciplines of Ayurveda
To keep the system of traditional Indian medicine alive.

A variety of facilities are also available for people like the traditional practice of panchakarma(cleansing of the body through 5 actions) and yoga therapy and anyone can book their appointment online. Several experienced teaching faculty are the specialty of the college. Adding to this, well-trained paramedical staff are 24 hours on duty at the college hospital for taking care of the patients. Though it has been only 8 years since the college was established, it has achieved a stone mile in almost every aspect. As of now the college takes admission for 100 students per batch and provides them with an exceptional learning environment.
Adding to this, the college is well equipped with spacious classrooms, a direction hall, a physiology lab, a central library, and all those facilities that a student requires. So now, let me take you on an amazing journey to Prasanna college of Ayurveda and hospital where we will get to know more about the college.

HIGHLIGHTS

This is exactly what the college believes in and this is why it promotes Ayurveda. Since Ayurveda has been long lost for the past few generations, the college is working towards educating our present generation about the importance of the same. Teachers at the college are well experienced with a teaching experience of more than 10 years in their discipline who not only teaches the students about the theoretical concept of the subjects but also about the practical knowledge which is very important for them as future physicians of Ayurveda. The college hospital provides the facilities like panchakarma, Abhyangam( full body massage), Kaya Seka( relieves body pain), Shirodhara(effective remedy for mental disorders), and Uttara Basti (gynecological ailments) to ordinary citizens as well and charges some minimal fee for the same. The experience of these facilities is so soothing since Ayurveda focuses on your spiritual mind and body which provides mental peace and relaxation.

MISSION & VISION

Mission statement: The college is dedicated to propagating the roots of the historical medical system of India called Ayurveda which is one of the oldest medicine systems to date that was forgotten by the people for many years. Adding to this the college mission is to impart quality education to its students and prepare them as ayurvedic physicians who are not only capable but also responsible both towards their career as well as society.

Vision statement: The college's vision is to spread the knowledge of Ayurveda to the younger generation of the country which was given to us by our ancestors as a part of our culture and heritage. The vision embodies the highest values and inspiration to the students so that they could rise above their weaknesses and look for their strength to be good human beings.

Faculty

The institute’s faculty always motivates them to take part in recreational and co-curricular activities. They have always motivated students to balance their academic and cultural activities since both are important for the overall development of the child. The teachers are well versed in the subjects they teach at the college and a student without any hesitation can reach them whenever he requires assistance.

The teaching faculty of the college is like a lighted lamp that always directs and guides its students about what steps to take further and is, therefore, they are the second parents of the child and of course their mentor.

    ADMISSION

    Eligibility Procedure
  • The candidate should have passed senior secondary(10+2) from a recognized board
  • They should have scored at least 50% marks with physics, chemistry, biology, and English as main subjects
  • Preference should be given to the person who knows Sanskrit
  • Should have completed 17 years of age on or before 31 December at the time of admission
  • Score in the neet entrance examination
    		•
  • Admission is based upon merit. The candidate needs to score 50% marks if he/she belongs to the general category.
  • For schedule caste, scheduled tribe and other backward classes the percentage required is 40%
  • The college then conduct counseling sessions for taking admission in the college

    CAMPUS

    Campus life is considered as the best part of anyone’s life where we make the most amazing and cheerful memories and learn a lot of things, not only related to academia but also related to our life and Prasanna ayurvedic college and hospital promises you the same zeal.it has a well-equipped physical infrastructure, spacious classrooms, clean surroundings, ample water facilities, playground, etc. The campus life of the college becomes an unforgettable moment for every graduate of the college. so let us look at the campus life of the institute

    INFRASTRUCTURE AND FACILITIES

    HOSTELS

    The college provides hostel facilities for both the boys and the girls. The college premises have 2 hostels separately away from the disturbance and nuisance of the society which helps children to concentrate on their studies after the lectures get over. The hostel area is under 24 hours surveillance and a help desk counter is also present in the hostel premise in case students need any help.

    TRANSPORTATION

    The college provides the students with transportation facilities for the movement of children back and forth from college to hostels and hospitals. The college buses are very spacious and the monthly charge for the same is around 800 to 1000. All the drivers are well experienced and have been working with the college from the time the college was established

    HERBAL GARDEN

    The institute has a spacious herbal garden with various types of medicinal flowers and herbs that are required by the students during their practical training and better learning as ayurvedic physicians. The herbal garden has a variety of medicinal plants that are used for teaching students in their day-to-day classes which helps them to gain practical knowledge.

    SPECIAL COACHING CLASSES

    At the time of the examination, the college provides the students with special coaching classes. The classes are compulsory for those students who are weaker in a particular subject and for others it is an option to attend or not. The teachers in the special classes help the children with the subject or any other particular topic that they can’t understand and this is one great initiative by the college.

    CENTRAL LIBRARY

    The college is equipped with a central library that provides a variety of books to the students from journals to magazines, periodicals to academic books. The college library is well furnished with a spacious central hall where the students can sit and read.

    FAQ

    How do I apply to Prasanna ayurvedic medical college?

    Students seeking admission in Prasanna have to qualify the basic requirement of clearing their neet examination conducted by NTA and then need to apply for admission for which the college conducts the counseling session, however, the preference is given to those students first who knows Sanskrit.

    How much is the fee for BAMS?

    The fee for BAMS is around INR 2,80,000 per year.

    Is Prasanna ayurvedic medical college private or the government?

    Prasanna ayurvedic medical college is a private un-aided college affiliated with Rajiv Gandhi institute of health and science and is governed by the central council of Indian medicine.

    What is the duration for BAMS?

    The duration of the course is 5 years with 1 year of compulsory internship at the hospital.

    Do they have hostel facilities at Prasanna medical college?

    Yes, the college provides hostel facilities to its students for both boys and girls. It has separate hostels with an accommodation capacity of around 150 students. The hostel fee is around 5000 per month.

    Is there any provision of scholarship to the students?

    Yes, the college provides various scholarships to talented and meritorious students. Scholarships and fee concession is also available to the students belonging to the EWS section of the society and other backward classes.
