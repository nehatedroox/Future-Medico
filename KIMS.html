OVER VIEW

Recognized by the medical council of India, the kempegowda institute of medical science is one of the most prominent medical colleges in India. It was established in the year 1980 by the vokkaligara sangha and is affiliated with the Rajiv Gandhi University Of Health And Science, Bangalore. Its foundation was laid down by the Honorable Chief Minister of Karnataka, Shri R Gundu Rao on 30 November 1980 which officially began conducting its classes from 16 march 1981, since then 16 march is observed as KIMS day every year. Spread over an area of 14 acres, Kempegowda institute of medical science is a privately held institution with an all-India ranking of 247. The institute not only offers MBBS courses under its UG and PG program to the students wanting to have a career in the medical field but also provides MD, MS, and diploma courses under different specializations. It has also been given recognition by various international councils like;

  • General Medical Council, United Kingdom
  • World Health Organisation
  • The royal college of obstetrics and gynecology, London
The institute focuses on providing quality education to its students and helping them develop a sense of responsibility and belongingness towards society as doctors. It primarily works towards developing his/her character and attitude which is required for professional life such as personal integrity, leadership quality, and showing love and care towards others.

How is Education at KIMS?

When it comes to education, KIMS aims at providing quality education to students to embark on a sense of responsibility and empathy towards society. Kempegowda Institute of medical science logo consists of a central wand with snakes wrapped around it which is a symbol of Greek and Roman gods of health and science. Its motto SATHATHA SEVA THATHPARATHE scripted in the Kannada language means “always devoted to service of mankind” clearly indicates that the primary focus of the college is to educate its student in such a way that every graduate of KIMS has a sense of sympathy and care towards mankind. The students are trained in such a manner that they not only become competent in the management of health problems as an individual or a community but also become familiarise with the nation's health programs and capable to work as leading partners in health care teams and gear up their knowledge to meet the health needs during natural calamities. Not only this but the institute also focuses on the social, cultural, and psychological needs of the children by enabling them to acquire good communication skills and continued self-learning.

Approvals and Accreditation

  1. The college is recognized by the medical council of India permanently for running MBBS courses since 1980-81
  2. It is recognized by the medical council of India for running postgraduate degree/diploma courses in various disciplines since 1991-92
  3. The college is affiliated with the Rajiv Gandhi University of Health and Science, Bangalore Karnataka
  4. It also has international recognition by the general medical council, United Kingdom, WHO, and the royal college of obstetrics and gynecology, London

DEPARTMENT (UG COURSE)

The Kempegowda institute of medical science offers a variety of UG courses to its students to specialize in. The various departments offered by the college are

Pre-clinical departments

  1. Department of Anatomy
  2. Department of Biochemistry
  3. Department of Physiology
During the first professional year, students learn these subjects. As a motivation to learn they get early clinical exposure which enhances basic science learning and helps them to have practical knowledge of their theoretical studies

Para Clinical Departments

  1. Department of Pharmacology
  2. Department of Pathology
  3. Department of Microbiology
During the second year of their ug course, the students are given an insight into clinical subjects. They are required to attend clinical postings in the hospitals after which they have to appear for their examination

Note: Both the departments are located at Banashankari 2nd Stage near BDA Complex, Bangalore-560070.

Clinical

Departments

After the successful competition of their second year, the students enter their 3 professional year part 1 in which they need to study subjects like Forensic Medicine & Toxicology, Ophthalmology, Otorhinolaryngology, and Community Medicine. After the completion of part 1 and before entering part 2 of the same year they are allotted a time of 2 months for elective postings. The student will rotate through two elective blocks each consisting of 4 weeks duration. This will provide flexible choices in student’s areas of interest, direct individual experience and also help students to develop self-directed learning skills.

    Admission Procedure

    For admissions to Kempegowda Institute of Medical Sciences, the candidate has to meet all the eligibility criteria for the respective course chosen.     FACILITIES

    Kims cooperative society -

    KIMS cooperative society which was started in the year 1981caters to the need of stationary of both students and staff. It provides them with various kinds of books, daily necessities, and other articles at a very reasonable price. The shareholders of the society consist of both the teaching faculty as well as the students.

    ATM/ Banks-

    To cater to the financial needs of the students, the campus has inbuilt banks like Punjab national bank and ATM facilities are also available 24*7. ATMs of corporation banks like the state bank of India, Canara bank, HDFC bank, axis bank are also situated in the nearby area surrounding the campus so that students have easy access to them whenever required.

    Transportation–

    The college also provides transportation facilities to its students from the college campuses to hostels and various training centers. There are a total of 5 buses including one minibus and 2 Maruthi vans

    Hostels

    The college provides 2 separate hostels for boys and girls to cater to their accommodation needs. The boys and girls hostels are located in the Institute campus at Banashankari with a total capacity of 200 students each. Student Welfare officers are always available to listen to any problems and grievances of the students, they are more like friends to them who are always ready to help them whenever required.

    Cafeteria

    The college campus provides 3 cafeterias to students which serve quality food at reasonable rates. The cafeteria at the college, hospitals and amenity area of the hospital campus is equipped with adequate seating capacity for both students and staff and has modern kitchen equipment. The third cafeteria not only caters to the need of food to the students but also provides them with a convenience store and also serves coffee and snacks for students.

    Sports room and gym

    The campus is very peculiar about the health and fitness of students and that’s why they provide the facility of gym and sports to the students within the campus. It has an exceptional health club fully furnished with all the gym equipment ranging from treadmills to steppers and a trained instructor to guide the students. The timing of the gym is 6:00 AM to 8:00 PM and the health club is open for every student of KIMS. It also provides the facilities for indoor games like chess, carrom, table tennis and much more. Besides this, the college is planning to open up a new sports complex which will be having 2 synthetic tennis courts, basketball and volleyball courts, and an amphitheater.

    FAQ

    How do I apply to Kempegowda institute of medical science?

    The procedure is quite simple and all you need to do is to be eligible for application i.e. you need to secure at least 50% marks in 10+2 with physics, chemistry, biology as the main subjects. The admission is based upon the marks secured by a candidate in the NEET examination

    How much is the fee for MBBS?

    The course fee for MBBS is around 10-15 lakhs per annum

    What are the career opportunities after MBBS?

    The student after obtaining the degree of MBBS can either work as a private practitioner or choose to practice as a general practitioner provided they are registered under any state government.

    Is KIMS a private college or the government?

    Kims is a privately held medical college in Bangalore Karnataka which is affiliated to Rajiv Gandhi University of health and science and registered under the medical council of India

    Do they have hostel facilities at KIMS?

    Kims provides basic facilities of accommodation to its students and has 2 hostels for both boys and girls separately with AC rooms and provides vegetarian as well as non-vegetarian food.
