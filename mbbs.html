MBBS (Bachelor of Medicine and Bachelor of Surgery) is the most popular and designed degree of doctors. Medical education calls for a profound change in the way it is taught and learned - a change that provides the welfare society needs, wherever a doctor practices. where a doctor can be recognized as a leader capable of introdu-cing such a transformation in an unstable world in which previouslyccontrolled diseases re-emerge and new ones arise, population aging increases at a rapid pace, systems face health coverage problems and increasing chronic diseases, and health public policy consumes much of our nation’s gross domestic product.
