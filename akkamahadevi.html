OVER VIEW

A well-known teaching institute for Ayurvedic Medicine and Practices. The institute cultivates and fosters talent in order to achieve excellence in research, teaching, service, and community development.

Akkamahadevi Ayurvedic Medical College and Research Centre is highly recognised for its comprehensive approach to teaching skills in such a way that students are well prepared to confront obstacles in their professional lives.

This institution, located in Bidar, Karnataka's north eastern city, offers a unique and creative approach to Ayurvedic education. The curriculum is designed in such a manner that current and relevant academic skills and hands-on experience are offered.

Akkamahadevi Ayurvedic Medical College offers every student with quality education, top-notch facilities, infrastructure, and ambiance, as well as instilling the values that every teaching faculty and staff carries to every student.

For prospective students in the health sciences, exposure to core academic strength and training beyond the topics and curriculum is why they like learning here. Students must have a solid foundation in their courses so that they can spread their wings as they advance in their careers. At Akkamahadevi Ayurvedic Medical College, a remarkable team of highly committed and experienced experts guarantees that students receive the best education possible.

COURSES OFFERED

BAMS (Bachelors in Ayurveda, medicine, and surgery)

The total intake capacity of Akkamahadevi Medical college is 60 and the seats are filled under 3 categories

  • 9 seats by AYUSH Admissions Central Counselling Committee (15% All India Quota)
  • 15 seats by Karnataka Examinations Authority under AYUSH Counselling (State Quota)
  • 36 seats under Management Quota Category

    • ADMISSION

    The admission procedure and eligibility criteria to apply for Akkamahadevi Ayurvedic Medical College is given below

    Course Eligibility Criteria Admission Process
    BAMS (Bachealors in Ayurvedic, Medical & Surgery) 10+2 with physics, chemistry, biology, and English as main subjects with at least 50% scores By appearing in the NEET examination.

    PROCEDURE

    TThe candidates seeking an undergraduate degree in BAMS at Akkamahadevi ayurvedic medical college can apply either for merit or management quota.

    The details are as follows::

  • 15% all-India merit quota

    • The candidates who have qualified for their NEET examination can apply for quota admission counseling which is conducted by none other than the AYUSH admission central counseling committee, ministry of AYUSH.

  • KEA counseling (Karnataka domicile only)

    • Karnataka examination authority(KEA) conducts counseling for the state domicile students for admission to 25% of the total seats in Akkamahadevi Ayurveda medical college

  • Management quota direct admission

    • Direct admission to college under management quota is based upon a first-come and first-serve basis. For further information and assistance, students are requested to contact the admission helpline of the college..

    The admission process of Akkamahadevi Ayurvedic College is pretty straightforward. The admission process is that the student must apply and pass the NEET exam with good marks in order to get into this institute.
