OVER VIEW

The gem of Jnana Prabha Campus of MG Charitable trust, The East Point College of Medical Sciences and Research Centre is located in Bidarahalli of the eastern part of Bangalore Urban District.
Under the aegis of Jnana Praha Campus, which includes East Point College of Medical Sciences and Research Center, Engineering and Tech, there are additional educational institutes ranging from technology to humanities and health sciences. This also includes College of Pharmacy, PU College and School, College of Management, and College of Nursing.
The extravagant campus with robust buildings surrounded by lush acres of landscapes overseeing the beautiful Bangalore cityscape.The essence and true spirit of the Garden city is encapsulated in this campus with large fields and lush greenery. A separate ecosystem that is far away from the hustle and bustle of the traffic and pollution of the city. The Eastern Point College and Hospital is surrounded by hundreds of villages that receive healthcare services, facilities and resources provided by the hospital, a gratuitous venture that is done without an iota of descrination or bias

Admission Procedure

The admission procedure at East Point of Medical Sciences and Research Centre may be completed in a few simple stages. Every medical student must take the National Eligibility and Entrance Examination (NEET), which is administered by the National Testing Agency's regulatory body (NTA). Candidates must sit for this test after completing their 12th grade in order to study MBBS in India. Candidates are provided common counselling on the colleges, universities, or institutes they can choose from based on their eligibility requirements, NEET score, rank, and available seats. These are the requirements for admission to any government, private, public, or deemed university.
