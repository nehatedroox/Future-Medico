OVER VIEW

Bachelor of Physiotherapy (BPT) is a four years undergraduate degree course for students who have passed their 10+2 or equivalent examination in the science stream. The degree is recognized by the Medical Council of India and the field of study is aimed at helping patients who have a partial or full disability of movement and patients recovering from injury to skeletal structure or soft tissues.
Physiotherapy is a process of carrying out treatment without the use of any drugs and only through the use of heat, electricity, mechanical forces, and mechanical pressure. The course involves six months of mandatory internship. The practitioners are required to have a good understanding of the human anatomy and the inter-working of bones, muscles, and nerves.

REQUISITES

1. Required Education.

Entrance examination at the state or national level like IPU CET, BCECE, VEE.
Candidates must have passed 10+2 or equivalent examination in Science stream with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology as compulsory subjects and 50% aggregate marks.
English proficiency.
Must be 17 years of age at the time of admission.

2. Projected Job Growth.

After completing the course you will have employment opportunities in Hospitals, Nursing Homes, Rehabilitation Centers, private clinics, and as well as freelancing.Due to the long working hours of today’s lifestyle, most people suffer from some type of mobility disorder after a certain age. At the same time, there are various spas, gymnasiums, health centers coming up in big cities and towns run by prominent hotel chains which provide ample job opportunities for professionals trained in physiotherapy. In this field, the professionals can earn anywhere between 4lakhs to 12 lakhs per year depending upon the location and organization.

3. Median Salary

The average Physiotherapist is expected to earn around 6lakhs per annum. After sufficient years of experience, the yearly earnings of an individual can go as high as 12-14 lakhs per year.

Program Guide

  • Program Details

    • Bachelor of Physiotherapy is a four-year course recognized by the Medical Council of Education where the students are taught topics related to manual therapy, exercises, and applications of electro – a physical model of treatment to treat skeletal and soft tissue damage. The course also involves a mandatory six-month-long internship and admission is provided on the basis of state level or national level entrance examinations. The course fees can range anywhere from 1lakh to 5 lakh depending upon the individual college.

Educational Requirements

1. Minimum of 50% aggregate marks in 10+2 or equivalent examination in the Science stream having Physics, Chemistry and Biology as compulsory subjects
2. Must qualify in the entrance examinations conducted at state and national levels.
3. Must have completed 17 years of age at the time of admission.
4. Must be proficient in English.

Skill Set Required
  1. Must have effective time and workload management skill
  2. Must have the ability to work in a diverse team
  3. Must have quick decision-making skills
  4. Must have written and oral communication skills, from working with patients and within a multi-disciplinary team
  5. Must have excellent communication and Interpersonal Skills
  1. Must have the ability to research and report writing
  2. Must have the ability to analyze research findings and to reflect on your practice.
  3. Must have the ability to use electro-physical modalities and other physical approaches in complex care practices.
  4. Must have an understanding of the types of exercise and movement used to help improve mobility and function
  5. Must have the knowledge of how to perform manual therapy safely
