OVERVIEW

Established in 2001 under the umbrella of Venkatesha Education Society, MVJ Medical College & Research Hospital is a well-established educational institute for imparting quality medical education. It is located in Hoskote which is a rural district well connected to the city. Equipped with the latest infrastructure, technology-enabled classrooms, and advanced laboratories the college primarily focuses on teaching, clinical practice, and research to train the next generation of doctors. In all specializations, the College offers MBBS, MD, MS, and Ph.D. programs. The college has received an ‘A' rating from NAAC and has been constantly rated among India's top medical colleges, including this year in 2021. The college curriculum is designed to provide doctors with a solid, well-rounded foundation of medical knowledge. Students follow a demanding academic schedule with a balanced focus on extra-curricular activities in order to develop professional-minded and scientifically temperate persons with an inquiry and reform attitude.

How is Education at MVJ Medical College and Hospital, Hoskote?

The teachers and the management of the MVJ Medical College and Research Hospital aim at providing value-based education to the students. The students are trained to become individuals who can transform society by making others socially conscious and responsible. The curriculum is crafted to teach students about health, development, human values, compassion, sympathy, sincerity, and honesty by focusing on learning by doing. The curriculum at MVJ is aimed at:

  • Meeting the regional and national health care needs in line with international standards.
  • Training students with the best tools and pedagogy emphasizing skills and service
  • Training students to bring out a paradigm shift in the community including providing affordable treatment for diseases.
  • Training students to follow Universal ethical values at work.

CAMPUS

Unlike most other medical colleges in the state, it is located in the center of Hoskote, the campus life engulfs students into an inspiring and fun-filled learning experience. Separate hostels for males and girls are well-furnished and well-maintained at the campus. The college campus is For the students, there are two dining halls where they may acquire food and refreshment four times a day. The institution also hosts a variety of cultural and academic events in which students can participate and make the most of their academic and dormitory experiences.

FACILITIES

The entire campus is covered with a lush green environment with modern and advanced facilities.

Hostel and Accommodation-

No less than a standard house with all the modern facilities, accommodation at MVJ MCRH offers an inspiring experience for every resident to live, study and excel. There are separate living dormitories for boys and girls each under 24/7 CCTV surveillance and proper security to take care of things. Out of 650+ rooms, 500 rooms are air-conditioned with a TV, Refrigerator, attached bathroom, and 24/7 power backup. Adding more to it, the entire hostel area is covered with multiple high-speed internet wifi connections and access to the central library of the college. When it comes to dining, the first thing the college administration has ensured is the hygienic and standards of the canteen. The elegant ambiance of the dining hall is well contributed with a full-fledged menu of north Indian and south Indian cuisines catering and satisfying the hunger of students from every part of the country. Starting from breakfast and all-time coffee/tea, the canteen offers lunch and dinner as well.

Sports & Recreation-

Placed at the highest priority, the college ensures the complete physical and mental well-being of the students carrying academic pressure. The college has some outstanding facilities and high-end opportunities when it comes to sports and activities. The campus exhibits football grounds, a cricket ground, provisions for tennis, badminton, and volleyball courts. The campus also has an open ground for all kinds of field events. The college has a well-maintained record of winning 4+ inter-college championships while organizing and participating in 10+ championships.

The campus also offers a modern gymnasium with professional trainers and advanced equipment. Students are highly encouraged to join the gym to enhance their physical fitness and remain active.

CENTRAL LIBRARY–

The college’s central library showcases a gigantic knowledge bank with a myriad of resources, books, research papers, and access to an array of e-journals and information portals for research. The entire library has single connectivity with an electronic automation system for the library handled by Easylib software. Spread at 35,000 sq ft on two floors on campus, the library can accommodate up to 850 people at a time. All resources are well organized and segmented as;


• U.G section • P.G section • Reference section • Journal section for National and International journals separately • Stock area • Back volume area • Periodical section • Dissertation section • Audio/video/CD’s section • Internet Section • Librarian office • Technical section • Common reading room • Separate faculty reading room The library’s main computer network has 50 nodes with sophisticated hardware infrastructure supported by a dedicated internet connection with high bandwidth. The central library at the college is registered to HELINET of RGHS allowing access to international medical journals and research papers.

FAQ

I want to join MVJ Medical College and Hospital. What are the admission criteria for MBBS?

Here admission will be provided on the basis of rank obtained in the NEET examination conducted by the Central Board of Secondary Education. Seats are allocated on a merit basis through counseling conducted by the Karnataka Examination Authority.

What is the admission procedure for foreign students?

The candidate must have passed the 10+2 examination, with 50% aggregate marks in Physics, Chemistry, and Biology, and had English as one of the subjects. The candidate must have documents to prove NRI/FOREIGN status. The candidate must have inward remittance.

How is MVJ Medical College and Research Hospital?

This is one of the best private medical colleges in India. It is ranked sixteenth overall. The College offers MBBS, MD, and MS courses in all specialties, PG Diploma, Ph.D. courses, and as well as Bachelors, Masters and Ph.D. degree in Physiotherapy. The college ranks 16th among all medical colleges of India at present in 2021. The college curriculum focuses on making doctors with a strong, comprehensive foundation of medical knowledge.

How much are the fees for MBBS?

The course fees for studying MBBS at MVJ are around Rs. 10 lakhs - Rs11 lakhs per annum excluding the miscellaneous charges.

What are the career opportunities after MBBS?

The students after completion of their MBBS degree can practice as general practitioners provided they are registered under a State government. The students can work as junior doctors in medical colleges and after passing out they can work as medical consultants, doctors with various hospitals, and private clinics. They can also work as private practitioners as well.

Do they have hostel facilities at MVJ Medical College and Hospital?

Yes. The college has separate hostels for boys and girls. The boy’s hostel has a capacity of 310 while the girl’s hostel has a capacity of 640. Both the hostels have well-furnished, high-class amenities to give the feel of home away from home.

How is the campus life at MVJ Medical College and Hospital?

The students get to enjoy the best campus life among any college in Hoskote. The hostel committee of each campus has made sure of all needed facilities for the best academic outcome. The dynamic campus life pushes the students towards healthier, focused, and with all modern education amenities. At the same time to ensure safety and discipline for all the students, the college has the necessary restrictions and rules in place which are well enforced by the hostel authorities. The college has separate clubs for literature, dramatics, fine arts, and other academic disciplines where the students can practice their hobbies and interests. The college organizes regular conferences and guest lectures by experts to further inculcate the rich academic culture required for the students to excel in their respective careers
