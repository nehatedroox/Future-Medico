OVERVIEW

Established on 1st June 1996 at Bangalore by the Govt. of Karnataka, the Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences is a premier Health Science University in India. It was established in Karnataka through the enactment of Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences, Karnataka Act 1994 to include and consider all the health science colleges and institutions earlier affiliated to the conventional universities in Karnataka. It was established with an aim of providing proper and systematic instruction, teaching, training, and research in modern medicine.

The university was established to meet the need for regulation, monitoring, and standardizing the curricula as well as the evaluation systems in medical studies due to the phenomenal increase in the number of institutions and students admitted to various courses of medicine and allied health sciences. The purpose of medical and health science education offered by Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences is to meet the societal needs for better healthcare. RGUHS had the vision of bringing uniformity in the standards of teaching and having a common curriculum for the various courses offered in different colleges across Karnataka. The medium of instruction in all the affiliated colleges under RGUHS is English.

The university is one of the top universities in India offering the best medical education which has always taken initiatives to make its curriculum and pedagogy for different courses of world-class quality and taking it to the forefront of medical education in India.

The management and faculty of the institution had always been trying to impart quality education to all the students coming from diverse backgrounds from all over the country. The college’s alumni are placed in the highest positions of the healthcare and pharmaceuticals sector. Here the curriculum is designed and crafted to help the students to learn and understand various attributes of becoming the best pharmaceutical practitioner. The students here are trained by a team of experienced lecturers and professors catering to learning opportunities encompassing the professional services as well as towards community healthcare.

How is Education at Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences, Bangalore?

Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences follows a curriculum that focuses on the delivery of healthcare in the context of community service and population service. The university aims to bring about research changes in the world by providing quality education. The university aims at training students who would look at health care in its widest sense not just focusing on treating illness and injury. The university strives to achieve excellence in education, research, and outreach for the protection, restoration, and promotion of health.
●Meeting the regional and national health care needs in line with international standards.
●Training students with the best tools and pedagogy emphasizing on skills and service
●Training students to bring out a paradigm shift in the community including providing affordable treatment for diseases.
●Training students to follow Universal ethical values at work.

PLACEMENT

AT RGHS, each student is prepared with the right guidance to prepare and accomplish a worthy career in the future. The students after completion of the degree in any medical department have tremendous job and career opportunities. The university has an in-house hospital allowing the students with hassle-free internship options with the best learning opportunities.

Studying popular courses like MBBS, BDS, and Allied Health Sciences can fetch job opportunities with lucrative salary options that can go up to 16-17 lakhs per annum. Also, the training department at the university assists the students in getting internships with stipends offered up to Rs. 40,000 per month.

Every year, students are assisted with ace recruitment options where the college administration continuously goes on catering job opportunities to graduated students.

FACULTY

To provide quality education to the students, the college employs highly experienced and competent faculty. All of the professors are Post Graduates in their fields, with more than half of them holding Doctorate degrees.

DIGITAL LIBRARY

The digital library at RGUHS is the first of its kind in the entire nation allowing the students to have access to a myriad of resources including relevant e-books, e-journals, databases, and a number of information portals for medical research. In 2003, the HELINET consortium was established to enhance the reach of resources by students and staff providing access to 7000+ online bibliographies UG & PG courses along with solved question papers for all medical exams.

2.How should I go about becoming a student of RGUHS?

First, decide the course you want to pursue. Next, find out the institutions offering the course and its location. Next, procure the details about the institutions from the institutions’ websites. Please be aware of unscrupulous elements who might claim to represent the University or Institution or offer help in transacting with them. It is therefore advised that the concerned should personally verify all aspects directly without any intermediaries.

3.How do I verify the college?

Find out about the college’s affiliation to the University for the concerned academic year on the Institutional and University website. If not found, please correspond to info@futuremedico.in. For other facilities in the chosen institution, Please access the institutional website.

4.When does the course commence?

The calendar of events indicating the commencement of course for every course offered by the University is available on the University website. The same is usually followed for every subsequent year. For confirmation, please email info@futuremedico.in.

5.What is the last date of admission?

The calendar of events indicating the last date of admission for every course offered by the University is available on the University website. More or less the same period is usually followed for every subsequent year. For confirmation, please email info@futuremedico.in.

6.How is the procedure for admission?

Find out about your eligibility to join the course. Obtain the eligibility certificate from the University. After that, complete the admission procedures by directly corresponding with the college authorities.

7.How do I obtain an eligibility Certificate?

Download the application form for the eligibility certificate of the foreign/NRI/SAARC category. Find out the category you belong to. Find out about your eligibility. Read the application form thoroughly and fill up all the information completely. Scan all the documents asked for and attach them. If the documents are in a foreign language except for English, the translated version should be certified by the respective Government of the country of the issuing authority. Pay the determined fees in equivalent Indian rupees through the bank. Also, enclose a letter of introduction by the embassy.
