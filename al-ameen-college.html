OVERVIEW

Al Ameen Medical College at the historic city of Bijapur was established in 1984 by Dr. Mumtaz Ahmed Khan, the founder of Al-Ameen Movements.Dr Mumtaz Ahmed Khan is an extraordinary man with a great vision and passion to improve society. He believed in the power of education and making sure all sectors of the society had access to the best facilities for education.Dr Mumtaz Ahmed Khan graduated from Stanley Medical College in Chennai and graduated from Madras University in M.S. He has contributed immensely in the field of education, Healthcare and other sectors like banking. A recipient of several awards like Karnataka Rajyothsava award (1990), Kempegowda Award, Junior Jayees Award and Public Relation Society of India Award, he is well known for generosity and philanthropic nature. He is a true social reformer with the aim to spread affordable and quality education throughout the county with the Al-Ameen Education Society and Al-Ameen Charitable Fund Trust.

Education at Al Ameen College

The education at Al Ameen college is structured and holistic. Al-Ameen Medical College is affiliated to Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences, Bangalore since 1994, the College has been accredited by the Medical Council of India. The institution has begun postgraduate programmes in several medical science disciplines, also MCI-recognized. The management and personnel of Al-Ameen Medical College look forward to making the institution one of our country's top medical facilities.

ADMISSION

The College offers Graduation and Post – Graduation courses such as MBBS, MD, and Diploma courses in Anatomy, Physiology, Microbiology, Pediatrics, Gastronomy and many other branches. The admission is given on the basis of NEET and other All India Examinations for various courses like BPT, BDS, Nursing and other courses. Some of the courses also require the candidate to appear for personal interview rounds. The entrance examinations are usually held around the month of May and June.

CAMPUS

The campus of Al Ameen Medical College is enormous and has over two hundred acres of land in the city of Bijapur in Karnataka. The college overlooks the western hills from this historic city. The college campus is serene and an incredible place to learn and grow. The college building, administrative block, recreation hall, the playground, basketball stadium, boys and girls hostel, central library, a main hospital with 500 beds and all the modern amenities and technologies that provide and care for people from Bijapur and all over the country. The facilities and the infrastructure in this campus enables students to make the most of it and get the opportunity to excel and be the best in their field.

INFRASTRUCTURE

ADVANCED LEARNING CENTER:

The Advanced Learning Center (ALC) has been established to provide hands-on training and organize workshops where students are taught basic and advanced surgical skills as well as teach specific methods of surgeries within orthopedics, ENT, invasive procedures, neurosurgery, general surgery It is always accessible by students, faculty and the nursing staff. It also organizes frequent workshops and implant validation programs.

OPERATION LABS:

There are two types of labs in the College.

  1. Medical Skills Lab- Here the students are taught the practical basics of surgery, life support, advanced cardiac life support, and other skills at post-graduate, graduate, and nursing levels using mannequins, models, and simulation techniques.
  2. Cadaveric Research and Training Unit– Here the students get to opportunity to have specific training to hone their practical skills

Is Al Ameen Medical college a good college to study in?

What is the fees structure for an MBBS course in Al Ameen Medical college?

What is the Eligibility criteria to apply for MBBS in Al Ameen Medical college?

How do I apply for Al Ameen Medical College

The admission process at Al Ameen Medical College is that every medical aspirant must take the National Eligibility and Entrance Examination (NEET), which is administered by the National Testing Agency's regulatory body (NTA). Candidates must sit for this test after completing their 12th grade in order to study MBBS in India. Based on their rank, NEET score, eligibility criteria and seats availability.
