OVERVIEW

Al Ameen Medical College at the historic city of Bijapur was established in 1984 by Dr. Mumtaz Ahmed Khan, the founder of Al-Ameen Movements. Dr Mumtaz Ahmed Khan is an extraordinary man with a great vision and passion to improve society. He believed in the power of education and making sure all sectors of the society had access to the best facilities for education.

Dr Mumtaz Ahmed Khan graduated from Stanley Medical College in Chennai and graduated from Madras University in M.S. The strive of Dr Mumtaz Ahmed Khan to shape and upgrade our society is incomparable.A humble and unassuming personality, he has made waves and created educational movements in South India with his strive and tenacity to ensure affordable educational facilities for all.

He has contributed immensely in the field of education, Healthcare and other sectors like banking. A recipient of several awards like Karnataka Rajyothsava award (1990), Kempegowda Award, Junior Jayees Award and Public Relation Society of India Award, he is well known for generosity and philanthropic nature. He is a true social reformer with the aim to spread affordable and quality education throughout the county with the Al-Ameen Education Society and Al-Ameen Charitable Fund Trust. The core of His dedication and strive has been transformed and seen in all educational institutions and learning centres from Al Ameen Charitable Fund Trust.

Education at Al Ameen College

Al Ameen college is the first Muslim Minority college with a full fledged teaching hospital affliated to it. Several schools and colleges including Arts, Science and Commerce colleges, Teacher Training Institute, Management Institution, Law College, College of Education, Dental College, Engineering College, Nursing School are also established through the Al Ameen Charitable Fund Trust.

Al-Ameen Medical College is affiliated to Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences, Bangalore since 1994, the College has been accredited by the Medical Council of India. The institution has begun postgraduate programmes in several medical science disciplines, also MCI-recognized. The management and personnel of Al-Ameen Medical College look forward to making the institution one of our country's top medical facilities.

MISSION & VISION

VISSIONThe vision of Al Ameen Medical College is to deliver high-quality education and resources with a global perspective. The members' commitment and efforts in this institute have transformed and can be seen in all educational institutions and learning centres run by Al Ameen Education Trust, as well as in Al Ameen Medical College.

MISSIONThe Al-Ameen Education Society and the Al-Ameen Charitable Fund Trust were established with the intention of elevating the impoverished and marginalised, particularly those from the Indian Muslim community, via high-quality medical care and education. With the appropriate supervision and exposure, Al Ameen Medical College's mission extends the variety of options for students seeking affordable medical education.

    ADMISSION

    The admission procedure at Al Ameen Medical College can be accomplished in a few simple stages. Every medical student must take the National Eligibility and Entrance Examination (NEET), which is administered by the National Testing Agency's regulatory body (NTA). Candidates must sit for this test after completing their 12th grade in order to study MBBS in India.

    Candidates are provided common counselling on the colleges, universities, or institutes they can choose from based on their eligibility requirements, NEET score, rank, and available seats. These are the requirements for admission to any government, private, public, or deemed university.

    EVENTS & CURRICULARS

    There are various events and co curricular activities that take place in this college. You can harness both your academic interests as well as your other interests and passions while pursuing this college.

    The clubs and associations in this college are ongoing and hustling with energy and enthusiasm.

    The curriculum at Al-Ameen Medical College is highly structured and methodical that is delivered by excellent faculties with top notch resources and infrastructure.

    Every MBBS student shall undergo a period of certified study extended over 4 ½ academic years. The first 12 months will include Phase I (pre Clinical) and no students will be allowed to enter the Phase II unless he/she clears Phase I. Para-clinical subjects will be taught with clinical subjects and last 18 months clinical subjects. All the courses are affiliated with Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences, Karnataka, Bangalore.

    MD/MS are 3 years programmes with dissertation training in several fields of medicine. There are 2 years without a dissertation in different disciplines. The "Journal Club" and "Seminar" are routinely presented to all Postgraduate students. In addition, during their course students have to be placed interdepartmentally. Intradepartmental examinations must be conducted at the end of each year.
