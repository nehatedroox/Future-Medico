OVERVIEW

Al Ameen Medical College at the historic city of Bijapur was established in 1984 by Dr. Mumtaz Ahmed Khan, the founder of Al-Ameen Movements.Dr Mumtaz Ahmed Khan is an extraordinary man with a great vision and passion to improve society. He believed in the power of education and making sure all sectors of the society had access to the best facilities for education.His mission was to uplift the poor and marginalised people especially from the Indian Muslim community. Dr Mumtaz Ahmed Khan graduated from Stanley Medical College in Chennai and graduated from Madras University in M.S. He has contributed immensely in the field of education, Healthcare and other sectors like banking. A recipient of several awards like Karnataka Rajyothsava award (1990), Kempegowda Award, Junior Jayees Award and Public Relation Society of India Award, he is well known for generosity and philanthropic nature. He is a true social reformer with the aim to spread affordable and quality education throughout the county with the Al-Ameen Education Society and Al-Ameen Charitable Fund Trust.Al-Ameen Medical College is the first Muslim Minority college with a full fledged teaching hospital affliated to it. Several schools and colleges including Arts, Science and Commerce colleges, Teacher Training Institute, Management Institution, Law College, College of Education, Dental College, Engineering College, Nursing School are also established through the Al Ameen Charitable Fund Trust.The strive of Dr Mumtaz Ahmed Khan to shape and upgrade our society is incomparable.Having 15 office locations across different areas of Karnataka, he is the Chief Promoter and the President of Amanath Co-operative Bank Ltd (Schedule). He is the founder and President of Al-Ameen Islamic Finance and Investment Corporation (India) Ltd. It is the first non-interest banking finance company in India to be established on the Principles of Shariah in 1985. There are eleven branches in India. He is the Chief Promoter of Bangalore's "The Daily Salary," an Urdu daily. He is also a sponsor of the Karnataka State Young Wrestlers Welfare Trust.A humble and unassuming personality, Dr Mumta Ahmed Khan has made waves and created educational movements in South India with his strive and tenacity to ensure affordable educational facilities for all.In 1979, the great visionary and philanthropist Dr. Mumtaz Ahmed Khan in Bangalore with a group of important Muslim individuals, formed a charitable organisation. The Al-Ameen Charitable Fund Trust aims to set up educational and welfare organisations and organisations for Muslim communities and other individuals beneath privileged circles, mainly for the benefit of Muslim communities. The district of Bijapur, where Muslim people are large, is educationally and economically poor. That is why in this ancient city of the Great Adil Shahis, the Medical College, the Dental College and the Nursing School were founded.





Education at Al Ameen College

The education at Al Ameen college is structured and holistic. Al-Ameen Medical College is affiliated to Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences, Bangalore since 1994, the College has been accredited by the Medical Council of India. The institution has begun postgraduate programmes in several medical science disciplines, also MCI-recognized. The management and personnel of Al-Ameen Medical College look forward to making the institution one of our country's top medical facilities.students are taken to frequent hospital visits whichgive them an insight into how the professional worldfunctions, thus motivating them to excel not only theoretically by cramming their textbooks but also practically as a professional doctor.

ADMISSION

The College offers Graduation and Post – Graduation courses such as MBBS, MD, and Diploma courses in Anatomy, Physiology, Microbiology, Pediatrics, Gastronomy and many other branches. The admission is given on the basis of NEET and other All India Examinations for various courses like BPT, BDS, Nursing and other courses. CAMPUS

Akash Institute of Medical Sciences and Research Centre, a brand new campus stretching over several acres, is located in Bangalore city, right near Kempegowda International Airport, and has a huge campus with an abundance of amenities and infrastructure. This college's campus is vibrant and joyful, with a strong growth environment. One of the best places to study and learn, with excellent materials that are easily available. A fully functional Affiliated teaching hospital is also located immediately close to the college campus. The hospital's infrastructure and facilities is in accordance with the terms and guidance provided by Medical Council of India (MCI) There are several blocks, including an administrative block, an inpatient block, an outpatient block, wards, a critical care unit, a casualty unit, and an emergency ward. There are distinct facilities and wards for males, women, and physically disabled patients. The hospital has a nurses duty room and a demonstration room for educational reasons. There is also a treatment and examination room, department offices, and teaching staff quarters.

ADVANCED LEARNING CENTER:

The Advanced Learning Center (ALC) has been established to provide hands-on training and organize workshops where students are taught basic and advanced surgical skills as well as teach specific methods of surgeries within orthopedics, ENT, invasive procedures, neurosurgery, general surgery It is always accessible by students, faculty and the nursing staff. It also organizes frequent workshops and implant validation programs.

OPERATION LABS:

There are two types of labs in the College.

  1. Medical Skills Lab- Here the students are taught the practical basics of surgery, life support, advanced cardiac life support, and other skills at post-graduate, graduate, and nursing levels using mannequins, models, and simulation techniques.
  2. Cadaveric Research and Training Unit– Here the students get to opportunity to have specific training to hone their practical skills

FAQ

How can I join Akash Medical Institute?

The process of joining Akash institute of Medical Sciences and Research Centre is straightforward. Medical aspirants must undertake the NEET examination, based on their rank and score, they would be eligible for common counselling where based on the seats available, they can choose amongst the colleges they can opt in the ranking range. This is the process to apply and join any government or Private medical college in India.

What is the fee of Aakash Institute for Medical?

The fees in this college ranges from 55.98 LAKHS for MBBS to 5.7 LAKHS for MD in Common Medicine or Pharmacology.

When did Akash medical college start?

Established under Akash Education & Development Trust, Akash Institute of Medical Sciences and Research Centre(AIMS&RC) was Founded in 2014 in Bangalore, Karnataka.

What are the admission criteria for MBBS in Aakash Medical College?

The application process for this is simple. Medical aspirants must take the NEET test, and depending on their rank and score, they will be qualified for common counselling, where they will be able to pick among the institutions in the ranking range based on the seats available. This is the procedure for applying to and being admitted to Akash Institute of Medical Sciences and Research Centre
