    ADMISSION

    Eligibility

    For admissions to BAMC&H, the candidate has to meet either of the following eligibility criteria;

  • The candidate who has passed 10+2 or equivalent education (PUC) from a recognized university or board with PCB and English as major subjects and has passing marks of a minimum of 50% in aggregate is eligible.
  • Or the candidate has qualified in BSc Part I degree examination from a recognized or statutory university securing 50% marks in subjects like Physics, Chemistry, and Biology or Zoology or Botany provided the candidate also must have met the basic eligibility for the PUC standard.
  • Or the aspirant has passed the BSc final degree examination with a minimum of 50% marks in subjects like Physics, Chemistry, and Biology or Zoology or Botany and must have English as one of the major subjects in PUC or equivalent exams.
  • As a mandate criteria, the aspirant must be aged 17 as of December 31st of the year of admission.
  • For students clearing examinations other than Karnataka board PUC, the candidate has to show an eligibility certificate issued by Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences.

    CAMPUS

    The campus at Bapuji Multi Speciality Hospital And Ayurvedic College, Shimoga offers an exciting and unique learning experience for students.     Multi-Speciality Hospital

    HOSTEL

    The college has on-campus hostel blocks, separate for boys and girls. The hostel offers accommodation for 60 boys and 150 girls featuring all advanced living facilities like wardrobes, study tables, chairs, and attached bathrooms, with up to two sharing options. Both hostel blocks are covered with 24/7 surveillance and security ensuring a safer stay. The hostel has a common dining area with a sitting area for 100 students at a time maintaining the highest standards of quality and hygienic surroundings. The hostel canteen offers only vegetarian food of both north and south Indian styles.

    FAQ

    To which university is Bapuji Multi Speciality Hospital And Ayurvedic College, Shimoga affiliated?

    BAMC&H is affiliated with Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences, Bangalore, Karnataka.

    Can I apply for Direct Admissions to Bapuji Ayurvedic Medical College?

    Yes, you can apply for direct admissions at Bapuji Ayurvedic Medical College through management or sponsored quota. Prior to applying the candidates need to clear all the eligibility for studying BAMS.

    What is the total course fee for BAMS?

    The total course fees for studying BAMS at BAMC&H is near about Rs. 5.80 lakhs for the complete courses schedule.

    What is the total intake of students for BAMS?

    At Bapuji Multi Speciality Hospital And Ayurvedic College, the total intake of students for BAMS is 50.

    What is the total tenure of BAMS Degree at Bapuji Medical College?

    The total tenure for completing a BAMS degree is 5.5 years with 4.5 years of the academic program and 1 year of mandatory internship.
