OVER VIEW

Founded in the year 1973 by Sri Adichunchanagiri Shikshana Trust (Regd.), BGS Global Institute of Medical Sciences, Bangalore aims to achieve the objective of rendering social, charitable, educational, and medical services along with awakening spiritualism among the people of Karnataka, India, and the NRI community across the world under the holy leadership of Sri Adichunchanagiri Math Jagadguru Sri Sri Sri Balagangadharanatha Maha Swamiji. The college has been providing Undergraduate and Postgraduate education in Pharmaceutical Studies for the last four decades.

The College offers MBBS, MD, and MS courses in all specialties, PG Diploma, Ph.D. courses, and as well as Bachelors, Masters and Ph.D. degree in Physiotherapy. The college curriculum focuses on making doctors with a strong, comprehensive foundation of medical knowledge. The students undergo a rigorous academic schedule with a balanced focus on extracurricular activities. The students are provided an opportunity to practice in well-equipped OTs as well as in the rural and urban field practice areas so that they learn to make decisions independently and collaborate with a fellow student of nursing, physiotherapy, or pharmacy.
The management and faculty of the institution had always been trying to impart quality education to all the students coming from diverse backgrounds from all over the country. The college’s alumni are placed in the highest positions of the healthcare and pharmaceuticals sector. Here the curriculum is designed and crafted to help the students to learn and understand various attributes of becoming the best pharmaceutical practitioner. The students here are trained by a team of experienced lecturers and professors catering to learning opportunities encompassing the professional services as well as towards community healthcare.

How is Education at BGS Medical College?

The teachers and the management of the BGS Medical College, Bangalore aim at providing value-based education to the students. The students are trained to become individuals who can transform society by making others socially conscious and responsible. The curriculum is crafted to teach students about health, development, human values, compassion, sympathy, sincerity, and honesty by focusing on learning by doing. The curriculum at BGS Global Institute of Medical Sciences is aimed at:

• Imparting superior quality of medical education in accordance with the international standards which are in sync with societal requirements and global relevance.
• Creating a conducive environment to promote research activities.
• Providing appropriate infrastructure and education to enable students to deliver quality health care service.
• Inculcating a sense of keenness for lifelong learning and a sense of social responsibility through community services.

PLACEMENT

The students after completion of their MBBS degree can practice as general practitioners provided they are registered under a State government. The students can work as junior doctors in medical colleges and after passing out they can work as medical consultants, doctors with various hospitals, and private clinics. They can also work as private practitioners as well.
The student’s diploma courses will have employment opportunities in Hospitals, Nursing Homes, Rehabilitation Centers, private clinics, as well as freelancing. There are various spas, gymnasiums, health centers coming up in big cities and towns run by prominent hotel chains which provide ample job opportunities for professionals trained in physiotherapy. In this field, professionals can earn anywhere between 4lakhs to 12 lakhs per year depending upon the location and organization. The average nurse, physiotherapist, or medical equipment handler is expected to earn around 6lakhs per annum. After sufficient years of experience, the yearly earnings of an individual can go as high as 12-14 lakhs per year

FACULTY

The college has very experienced and highly qualified faculties to impart quality education to the students. All the faculties are Post Graduates in their respective professions and more than half of them hold Doctorate degrees. There are 380 staff nurses and 180 non-teaching staff members to assign the students in their learning process.

CAMPUS

The students get access to the best campus life of any Bangalore college. Each campus's hostel committee has ensured that all necessary facilities are available for the greatest academic results. The dynamic campus life pushes the students towards healthier, focused, and with all modern education amenities. At the same time, the institution has the appropriate limits and guidelines in place to protect the safety and discipline of all students, which are strictly implemented by the hostel officials. Students can also stay in well-kept and reasonably priced hostels at the college.
For the convenience of the students, the college has criteria and a well-equipped library that is open every day. The college has two basketball and one volleyball court, therefore there are plenty of chances for sports. The seventy-acre campus has a food street that serves food from various food joints within a considerable budget.

FACILITIES

HOSTEL:

The college has separate hostels for boys and girls. The hostels provide separate study rooms for each student, common rooms, and attached bathrooms. Both the hostels have well furnished, high-class amenities to give the feel of home away from home.

SPORTS AND GYMNASIUM:

The college has a hydraulic gymnasium on campus where there are upper body fitness machines separately and lower body machines separately, three professional self automatic treadmill machines, professional bicycles, two sets of dumbbells(from low weight to high), and barbells of all weights.
The gymnasium has the capacity to provide fitness training to teachers and students alike. The gym remains open for six hours a day at different times for males and females. The college also organizes outdoor and indoor sports and cricket matches every weekend.

CENTRAL LIBRARY:

The college’s central library is one of the major attractions for students on the campus. Established in the year 2012 - 2013, the library is home to 15000+ medical books as well as the library has an exclusive subscription to 125 yearly periodicals including leading medical journals and subjects.
Contributing to the congenial atmosphere for learning and growth, the library ensures quick and dedicated responses catering to the needs of the students as well as the staff members. There are even facilities for photocopying, along with separate reading rooms for students to relax and study in a serene environment. The library is open for students from 8:am to 10:pm every working day.
BGS GIMS Library is a member of the HELINET Consortium allowing the students to leverage top-quality journals, medical periodicals, and e-books with full support for thesis and dissertations.

INFRASTRUCTURE

ADVANCED LEARNING CENTER:

The Institute has state-of-the-art air-conditioned lecture theatres which are designed to accommodate 250 students at a time. The lecture halls are equipped with the latest audio-visual technology which includes LCD projectors, microphones, projectors, slides, etc. The individual departments have seminar rooms used for the purpose of seminars and practical demonstrations with a seating capacity of 25 to 40 with audio-visual aids.
There are also digital cameras installed to record live lectures, which are converted into digital format and uploaded in the Learning Management System to facilitate E-learning. Multi-purpose halls are used for conducting internal and university exams which are equipped with a surveillance system to monitor the students during the examination.

OPERATION LABS:

There are two types of labs in the College.

  1. Medical Skills Lab-
    Here the students are taught the practical basics of surgery, life support, advanced cardiac life support, and other skills at post-graduate, graduate, and nursing levels using mannequins, models, and simulation techniques.
  2. Cadaveric Research and Training Unit–
    Here the students get to opportunity to have specific training to hone their practical skills.
