OVER VIEW

Situated in Sindagi, Bijapur district of Karnataka, TSPM AYURVEDIC MEDICAL COLLEGE is one of its kind which not only believes in imparting quality education to the students but also trains them to become healers in the future. TSPM aims to produce research and development centres in Ayurveda at the international level and is driven towards popularising Ayurveda through skilled education, development and research.

Established in 1997 the institute is managed by Taluka Shikshana Prasaraka Sahakar Mandal and offers a bachelor’s degree to students across the nation who want to study Ayurveda as their career option. Since the historic root of the Indian medicine system “Ayurveda” has lost its importance in today’s time, the college works towards keeping this treasure of our nation safe and alive.

As of now, the college is working in the various domains to build up its foundation ranging from collaborating with universities abroad, conducting seminars at the national and international level to establishing the most effective flavouring garden for research orientation and preparation of medication for distributing to the poor and needy along with exporting it worldwide which will ultimately contribute towards the GDP of our country.

Adding to this, the college is considered as one of the best ayurvedic colleges in Bijapur which is committed to lifting the healthcare education as well as the healthcare system of our country. Moreover, the college has an impressive instructional atmosphere that motivates students to study and supports them across a broad and wide variety of ayurvedic disciplines.

HIGHLIGHTS

As mentioned by the local people, it is very clear that TSPM ayurvedic medical college is one of the finest colleges in its area that not only provides quality education to the students but also caters to the needs of the poor and needy people by providing health care services.

It is affiliated with Rajiv Gandhi institute of health and science and is registered under the central council of Indian medicine.

The college was established with the objective of fortification of rural people and upliftment of the rural healthcare system. The college is working towards popularising Ayurveda which is considered as one of the historic and traditional medicine systems which are not only followed in India but slowly is gaining popularity worldwide as well.

Adding to this the college has a vast campus and several facilities are available to the students like hostels, canteen, library, transportation etc.

MISSION & VISION

Mission statement: The mission is to provide an exceptional and impressive instructional atmosphere that supports study across a wide domain so that the long lost traditional Indian medicine system of Ayurveda can once again rejuvenate and attain a level of being a serious and educational health profession.

Vision statement: The college vision is the same as those of any other ayurvedic college i.e to spread the historic root of Ayurveda, the Indian system of health care across the nation and popularising it more and more through skilled education, research and development. It also includes establishing their research and development department at various international levels for manufacturing and distributing ayurvedic medicine across the globe.

Faculty

The college not only believes in educating children through books and becoming well educated; but cares that they also become wise and responsible as future doctors. The college believes that each child is unique in his or her way and is, therefore, a progressive thinker. Teachers at TSPM do not teach the students instead they do self-study with them and this is how the bond between the two gets stronger and deeper. All the faculty members at the college are well experienced and educated who not only teaches students what is written inside the books but tells them what life is and what are their duties towards society. Adding to this the teachers also motivates students to take part in various recreational activities organised by the college like national and international seminars since it will ultimately help in the overall development of the child. As of now, the institute’s faculty along with the talented young minds of the country is working upon making their website that will profit the scholars, patients and research abroad.

    FAQ

    How do I apply to TSPM ayurvedic medical college?

    Students seeking admission in TSPM has to qualify the basic requirement of clearing their neet examination conducted by NTA and then need to apply for admission either through merit or management quota

    How much is the fee for BAMS?

    The fee for BAMS is around INR 2,00,000 per year.

    Is TSPM ayurvedic medical college private or the government?

    TSPM ayurvedic medical college is a private un-aided college affiliated with Rajiv Gandhi institute of health and science and is governed by the central council of Indian medicine.

    Do they have hostel facilities at TSPM medical college?

    Yes, the college provides hostel facilities to its students for both boys and girls. It has separate hostels with an accommodation capacity of around 150 students. The hostel fee is around 5000 per month.

    Is there any provision of scholarship to the students?

    Yes, the college provides various scholarships to talented and meritorious students. Scholarships and fee concession is also available to the students belonging to the EWS section of the society and other backward classes.
