OVER VIEW

Bachelor of Homeopathic Medicine & Surgery or BHMS is five years and 6 months integrated undergraduate course that involves learning and expertise in homeopathic medicine and related surgery. The 5.5 years course schedule is segregated into 4.5 years of academic learning and 1 year of mandate rotational internship. Most of the colleges have hospitals within the campus and engages their alumni for internships in houses.
Given the trending rise in contemporary medicine apart from the regularly used allopathic medicine, the popularity and demand of Homeopathic & Ayurvedic medicine are whooping worldwide. Homeopathic, unlike allopathy, works on leveraging the natural healing power of the body and the medication process works more in healing internally for a lifelong impact.
Studying BHMS in 2021 involves a balanced course module including several practical sessions alongside the theoretical learning of homeopathic texts. Students are made to learn the roots of medicine and surgery.

REQUISITES

1. Required Education.

The basic requirement for applying to an undergraduate course in BHMS, the candidate must have passed 10+2 or equivalent education with Physics, Chemistry, and Biology as the core subjects.

2. Projected Job Growth.

The course proffers a lucrative career scope with high job opportunities as well as entrepreneurial opportunities. Graduates can apply in private and government homeopathic hospitals as health consultants, doctors, therapists, physicians, etc.

3. Median Salary

A bit ahead of studying Ayurveda, a graduate in homeopathic medicine and surgery is offered an average salary of Rs. 40,000 - Rs. 60,000 to start with.

Program Guide

  • Program Details

    • BHMS course syllabus is mostly segmented for 4.5 years of academic sessions. The academic session includes a combination of subjects of equal importance on Physiology and the Anatomy of the human body. The program mostly involves parallelly imparted theoretical and practical sessions for students to help them learn more effectively. After finishing the academic session, the candidate has to attend a compulsory internship for a year (rotational depending on the university).

Admissions

Educational requirements and admission

To apply for BHMS in any of the private or government colleges in India, the aspirant has to comply with the following eligibility criteria; The candidate must have passed 10+2 or equivalent examinations with aggregate marks of 50% - 60% in Physics, Biology, and Chemistry as compulsory subjects. The candidate must be a minimum of 22 years of age (general category) and 24 years (SC/ST./OBC category students) by 31st December of the applying year. Admission Process

There are two ways to get admission to any college for BHMS once the candidate has fulfilled the eligibility criteria. Admissions through entrance exams: For almost a majority of colleges and universities, Admission for BHMS in 2021 is based on the performance of the candidate in state/national level entrance exams. The common entrance exam accepted nationwide is NEET. Once the entrance exam scores are accepted by the college, the candidate is called in for the next stages of the admission process including group discussion followed by a personal interview. After completion of the above-mentioned process, the college administration will go through all the supportive documents and grant admission based on final scrutiny. Direct Admission: Some universities and colleges also allow direct admissions for BHMS courses based on the performance and marks of the students in class 10+2 or equivalent examination.

Entrance Examinations

Among all the state and national level entrance exams accepted for admissions into the BHMS course, the candidate must apply for NEET 2021.

Salary Statistics and Employment outlook

A graduate in Homeopathic medicine and surgery not only has some of the lucrative career opportunities in India but globally. Studying in top colleges in India brings opportunities for higher education as well as job prospects abroad as well. An aspirant with a BHMS degree is reputed as a doctor and is eligible to work in any private or government healthcare sector with a homeopathy department. Such a doctor is also holding eligible to perform as a private practitioner with their homeopathic clinic. With the rising demand for homeopathic medicine and natural healing ways over allopathic medicine, many national and multinational organizations keep looking to hire BHMS graduates not only as a doctor but also in the pharmacy department and as healthcare supervisors. Salary: It is well known that every medical branch provides some lucrative salary packages. In the government sector, a BHMS graduate can be offered an average salary of Rs. 35,000 while in the private sector, the candidate can start with Rs. 20,000 to Rs. 25,000. With more experience, the average salary in the field can go up to Rs. 70,000 - Rs. 80,000.
