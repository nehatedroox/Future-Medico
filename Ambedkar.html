OVER VIEW

Established by Ananda Social and Educational Trust, the Dr. B.R.Ambedkar Medical College (RMC) is affiliated to the Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences (RGUHS), Karnataka, and recognized by the Medical Council of India (MCI) with the vision of providing quality education to all, maintain and set new standards in medical education and train doctors to be compassionate and focus on patient-centered healthcare. Equipped with the latest infrastructure, technology-enabled classrooms, and advanced laboratories the college primarily focuses on teaching, clinical practice, and research to train the next generation of doctors.
The College offers MBBS, MD, and MS courses in all specialties. The college also offers courses in Physiotherapy and Allied Health Sciences. The college ranks among the top medical colleges of India at present in 2021. The college curriculum focuses on making doctors with a strong, comprehensive foundation of medical knowledge. The students undergo a rigorous academic schedule with a balanced focus on extracurricular activities. The students are provided an opportunity to provide quality patient care without compromising medical excellence.

How is Education at Dr. B.R.Ambedkar Medical College, Bangalore?

Dr. B.R.Ambedkar Medical College's faculty and administration strive to provide students with value-based education. The pupils are taught to be socially conscious and responsible persons who may help to alter society. By emphasizing learning by doing, the curriculum aims to teach kids about health, development, human values, compassion, sympathy, sincerity, and honesty. Lectures, tutorials, and practical internships at the Hospital are used to teach the courses to students.

The curriculum at Dr. B.R.Ambedkar Medical College is aimed at:
  • ● Focusing on student’s learning helps them understand the role of each discipline in diagnosis and treatment.
  • ● Providing and upskilling students to handle appropriate infrastructure and education to enable students to deliver quality health care service.
  • ● Inculcating a sense of keenness for lifelong learning and a sense of social responsibility through community services.

PLACEMENT

Because this is a medical institution, there is no formal mechanism for students to be placed, and the college does not have placement cells like other colleges. After completing their MBBS degree, students can work as general practitioners if they are registered with the state government. Students can work as junior doctors at medical schools and then as medical consultants, doctors in various hospitals, and private clinics following graduation. They can also work as independent practitioners.
Students pursuing BPT and Allied Health Science degrees, on the other hand, will find work in hospitals, nursing homes, rehabilitation centers, private clinics, and as freelancers. Spas, gyms, and health facilities run by major hotel chains are springing up all over the country, providing plenty of career prospects for physiotherapists. Professionals in this sector might make anywhere from 4 lakhs to 12 lakhs per year, depending on the region and company. Nurses, physiotherapists, and medical equipment handlers are estimated to earn roughly 6 lakhs per year on average. The students also get the option of being employed as house staff under respective departments of the college. The students also get one and half years of internship after completing four years of study where they get a stipend of Rs 40,000 on average

FACULTY

The college has very experienced and highly qualified faculties to impart quality education to the students. All the faculties are Post Graduates in their respective professions and more than half of them hold Doctorate degrees.

Admission:

The college offers Graduation and Post – Graduation courses such as MBBS, MD, and MScourses in Anatomy, Physiology, Microbiology, Pediatrics, Gastronomy, and many other branches. Admission to Dr. B.R.Ambedkar Medical College is given based on NEET and other All India Examinations for BPT and Allied Health Sciences. Some of the courses also require the candidate to appear for personal interview rounds. The entrance examinations are usually held around the month of May and June.

CAMPUS

The students get to enjoy the best campus life among any college in Bangalore. The hostel committee of each campus has made sure of all needed facilities for the best academic outcome. The dynamic campus life pushes the students towards healthier, focused, and modern education amenities. At the same time to ensure safety and discipline for all the students, the college has the necessary restrictions and rules in place which are well enforced by the hostel authorities. The college also has well-maintained and affordable hostel facilities for students.
The college has criteria and a well-equipped library which remains open every day for the convenience of the students. There are also ample opportunities for sports with the college having two basketball and one volleyball court. The seventy-acre campus has a food street that serves food from various food joints within a considerable budget.

FACILITIES

TRANSPORT FACILITY–

The college maintains a fleet of buses to transport students and faculty members from their residences to the college campus while providing services at nominal charges. The college also uses these buses to take students on industrial visits and medical internships.

CENTRAL LIBRARY–

The college has a central library that contains 16,500 books along with microfilms, audio-visual aids, and internet connectivity. The library has two separate blocks which remain open 24 hours a day. The users have full access to current research tools and technologies and at the same time being accessible to students, residents, and staff. The library is a part of the “HELINET” consortium offered by RGUHS which provides access to more than 675 indexed national and international journals. The library has sixty-two computers with internet access and facilities for scanning, printing, and photocopying.

CAMPUS LIFE

Being situated in the heart of Bangalore, the students here get to enjoy the best campus life unlike most other medical colleges of the state. The college has well furnished and well maintained separate hostels for boys and girls. There are also two dining halls for the students where they can get food and refreshment four times a day. The college also organizes various cultural and academic events where the students can take part and make the most of their academic as well as hostel life.

HOSTEL-

The college has separate hostels for boys and girls. All the rooms have color television, attached bathroom, and other facilities. The rooms are of two types, single and double bed occupancy types. Both the hostels have well-furnished, high-class amenities to give the feel of home away from home.
