OVER VIEW

N.K. Jabshetty Ayurvedic Medical College & Post Graduate Centre, Bidar is a world-class centre for Ayurvedic Medical Education and Research. It was founded by Sri Chidambara Shikshana Samsthe and led by Param Poojya Sri Sri Shiva Kumar Swamiji of Sri Siddharudh Math, with the goal of providing professional education and enriching students. This institute aims to provide effective health care for the suffering people of this region, and to spread the Indian System of Medicine- Ayurveda with the motto "Health for All."

On September 19, 1991, the Honorable Speaker of the Lok Sabha, Sri Shivraj Patil, inaugurated the institution.
The BAMS Undergraduate Program started in 1991-92 with a large number of students.In the academic year 2000-2001, the institute established the Post Graduation programme, i.e., MD/MS in Ayurveda. The postgraduate programme began with two specialisations: MD in Rachana Sharir (Anatomy) and MS in Shalya Tantra (Surgery). This programme, which began with only 5 students in each department, is now fully equipped with all facilities and resources for students from all over the nation. With new specialities introduced in recent years, this school now has 30 seats accessible for P.G. courses.

N.K. Jabshetty Ayurvedic Medical College & Post Graduate Centre, Bidar guarantees that the best resources and techniques are used to provide the highest quality of education The institution's approach is high quality education with a core of Ayurvedic basics and a contemporary and scientific viewpoint and methodology. This is the strategy that has resulted in outstanding performance by postgraduate and undergraduate students in the medical service industry. Every student at this institute may attain high success rates in their career and professional lives with the help of outstanding mentors and top-notch facilities.

    Course Eligibility Criteria Admission Process
    BAMS The total intake capacity of NK Jabshetty Ayurvedic Medical College and PG Centre, Bidar is 60 seats for UG courses and 30 for PG courses.

    The admission procedure and eligibility criteria to apply for N.K. Jabshetty Ayurvedic Medical College & Post Graduate Centre, Bidar is given below.
  • For the Undergraduate course, BAMS at N.K. Jabshetty Ayurvedic Medical College & Post Graduate Centre, Bidar - The candidate should have passed 10+2 with physics, chemistry, biology, and English as main subjects with at least 50% scores to be eligible for appearing in the NEET examination.
  • For postgraduate, A candidate who has passed the final year B.A.M.S. examination after pursuing a BAMS course in an Ayurveda Medical College recognised by the Central Council of Indian Medicine, or from a recognised Ayurveda Medical College, or affiliated with any other University, recognised as equivalent thereto by RGUHS. Candidates must have also completed a one year compulsory internship in the teaching institution or a hospital.

    PROCEDURE

    The candidates seeking an undergraduate degree in BAMS at N.K. Jabshetty Ayurvedic Medical College & Post Graduate Centre, Bidar can apply either for merit or management quota.

    The details are as follows::

  • 15% all-India merit quota

    • The candidates who have qualified for their NEET examination can apply for quota admission counseling which is conducted by none other than the AYUSH admission central counseling committee, ministry of AYUSH.

  • KEA counseling (Karnataka domicile only)

    • Karnataka examination authority(KEA) conducts counseling for the state domicile students for admission to 25% of the total seats in N.K. Jabshetty Ayurvedic Medical College & Post Graduate Centre, Bidar

  • Management quota direct admission

    • Direct admission to college under management quota is based upon a first-come and first-serve basis. For further information and assistance, students are requested to contact the admission helpline of the college..
