OVER VIEW

Master of Dental Surgery (MDS) course is a master level degree for students who have graduated in Dental Science. MDS degree as recognized by the Dental Council of India (DCI) is offered in various specializations including Prosthodontics and Crown & Bridge, Periodontology, Conservative Dentistry and Endodontics, Oral & Maxillofacial Surgery, Orthodontics, and Oral Medicine and Radiology.
Dentistry is a part of Medicines that will stop the disease effects occurring on the teeth. It is two years for those who have a Postgraduate Diploma to be recognized under DCI. Students who opt and improve their research skills in the dental area should go for an MDS Course.

REQUISITES

1. Required Education.

50% minimum marks in BDS.
Entrance examination at state or national level

2. Projected Job Growth.

After you have completed the MDS Course and study, you will earn a good package as the job profiles listed above. For different job profiles, there are different packages. Yet, the average salary package lies somewhere between Rs. 6 to 10 lakhs in a year. But, for a surgeon, the package lies between Rs.15 to 20 lakhs in a year.

3. Median Salary

As MDS graduates have a lot of value, their salary is also expected to be high. MDS graduate's average salary is INR 8 Lakh per annum.

Program Guide

  • Program Details

    • MDS is a 3 years post-graduation course after BDS that deals with the study of development, anatomy, and diseases of the teeth. Students who wish to do research related to teeth can opt for MDS after BDS. The MDS program promotes the development of critical analysis, research skills, and advanced clinical skills to improve the student in the field of dentistry.

  • Online Degree

    • It is possible to become a dentist by taking online courses, in choice areas or degrees. After this, one must obtain certification from the necessary regulating bodies.

Educational Requirements and Admissions

1.minimum marks in the qualifying degree examination for admissions to MDS course, 55% marks in aggregate of all subjects in BDS course.
2.You must be at least 21 years old to take admission in the MDS course. Hence you can apply for the entrance test of AICET.

NEET MDS entrance examination is held, and you need to cover its criteria. Clear the examination, and you shall be liable to enter the MDS Course.
