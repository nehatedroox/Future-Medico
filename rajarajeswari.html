OVER VIEW

Established in 1992 by the Moogambigai Charitable and Educational Trust, the Raja Rajeshwari Medical College and Hospital is affiliated to the Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences (RGUHS), Karnataka, and recognized by the Medical Council of India (MCI).with the vision of providing quality education to all. Equipped with the latest infrastructure, technology-enabled classrooms, and advanced laboratories the college primarily focuses on teaching, clinical practice, and research to train the next generation of doctors. The College offers MBBS, MD, MS, and Ph.D. courses in all specialties. The college has been accredited by NAAC with an abased examination AnesthesiA' grade and has ranked consistently among the top medical colleges of India including at present in 2021. The college curriculum focuses on making doctors with a strong, comprehensive foundation of medical knowledge. The students undergo a rigorous academic schedule with a balanced focus on extra-curricular activities to create professional-minded and scientific-tempered individuals with the spirit of inquiry and reform.

How is Education at KIMS?

Offering some of the popular courses like MBBS, MS, MD, PhD, BSc, Diploma, DM and MCh, studying at RajaRajeshwari Medical College is like a dream come true. The college’s dynamic learning and education environment is reflective of the senior most expert faculty and department heads. The college is attached with a 15000 beddings multi speciality hospital providing ample provisions and on campus cadavers for profound learning. Students aspiring to become a doctor are supported with a lush green and serene campus life ensuring a motivated and healthy environment.

● Meeting the regional and national health care needs in line with international standards.
● Training students with the best tools and pedagogy emphasizing skills and service
● Training students to bring out a paradigm shift in the community including providing affordable treatment for diseases.
● Training students to follow Universal ethical values at work.

Admission

The college offers Graduation and Post – Graduation courses such as MBBS, MD, MS, and Ph.D. The admission is given based on NEET and other All India. Some of the courses also require the candidate to appear for personal interview rounds. The entrance examinations are usually held around May and June.

Exams

The primary entrance exam accepted by the college is NEET. Admissions are granted based on the merit of the aspirant and the performance in NEET. For undergraduate courses, the candidate has to clear the cutoff in NEET UG and for post graduate students the candidate has to sit and crack NEET PG.

Faculty

The college is well equipped with 506 skilled and expert faculty with a remarkable experience in their field. Almost every HODs’ and faculty member has some of the highest post graduation doctorate degrees. The teachers are highly collaborative and cooperating encouraging students to pursue their higher education in medicine and further engage into research and development.

PLACEMENT

As a medical college, RajaRajeshwari has a proven track record of delivering talented doctors to the world. With global presence of alumni passing out from Rajarajeswari medical college, every graduate is placed in a majority of the renowned companies, hospitals, healthcare companies with a lucrative package offer and highest dignity.

An MBBS graduate can practice as general practitioners provided they are registered under a State government. The students can work as junior doctors in medical colleges and after passing out they can work as medical consultants, doctors with various hospitals, and private clinics. They can also work as private practitioners as well.

Given to the inhouse multispeciality hospital at RajaRajeswari Medical College, each student has the option of hasslefree internship support.

Approvals and Accreditation

The college is accredited with a rank ‘A’ by NAAC along with HLACT, ISO, IAO, and NABL. The hospital got its recent accreditation confirmed by NABH.

    KIMS cooperative society which was started in the year 1981caters to the need of stationary of both students and staff.     Hostels

    The college provides 2 separate hostels for boys and girls to cater to their accommodation needs. The boys and girls hostels are located in the Institute campus at Banashankari with a total capacity of 200 students each. Student Welfare officers are always available to listen to any problems and grievances of the students, they are more like friends to them who are always ready to help them whenever required.

    Cafeteria

    The college campus provides 3 cafeterias to students which serve quality food at reasonable rates. The cafeteria at the college, hospitals and amenity area of the hospital campus is equipped with adequate seating capacity for both students and staff and has modern kitchen equipment. The third cafeteria not only caters to the need of food to the students but also provides them with a convenience store and also serves coffee and snacks for students.

    The campus is very peculiar about the health and fitness of students and that’s why they provide the facility of gym and sports to the students within the campus. Besides this, the college is planning to open up a new sports complex which will be having 2 synthetic tennis courts, basketball and volleyball courts, and an amphitheater.

    FAQ

    How do I apply to Kempegowda institute of medical science?

    The procedure is quite simple and all you need to do is to be eligible for application i.e. you need to secure at least 50% marks in 10+2 with physics, chemistry, biology as the main subjects. The admission is based upon the marks secured by a candidate in the NEET examination

    How much is the fee for MBBS?

    The course fee for MBBS is around 10-15 lakhs per annum

    What are the career opportunities after MBBS?

    The student after obtaining the degree of MBBS can either work as a private practitioner or choose to practice as a general practitioner provided they are registered under any state government.

    Is KIMS a private college or the government?

    Kims is a privately held medical college in Bangalore Karnataka which is affiliated to Rajiv Gandhi University of health and science and registered under the medical council of India

    Do they have hostel facilities at KIMS?

    Kims provides basic facilities of accommodation to its students and has 2 hostels for both boys and girls separately with AC rooms and provides vegetarian as well as non-vegetarian food.
