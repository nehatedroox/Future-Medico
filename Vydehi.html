OVER VIEW

Established in the year 2000, the Vydehi Institute of Medical Sciences and Research Centre is situated in Bangalore. It is spread in a lush green pleasant and peaceful atmosphere. It is affiliated with Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences and approved by the Dental Council of India, and Medical Council of India. It is recognized as one of the leading medical institutes progressive to provide health care services and education with global excellence. Having highly qualified, experienced, and dedicated faculty, the institutes prioritize hands-on learning, with the best quality, comprehensive, and integrated medical education using modern technology and research. The institute also has a world-class integrated healthcare system, with a focus on sensitive patient care to underprivileged people. Its mission is to bring multinational healthcare standards within the reach of everyone.

How is education at Vydehi Medical College and Research Center, Bangalore?

Vydehi Medical college and research center provides efficient and expert teaching faculty, world-class infrastructure, wide and digital classrooms, specious laboratories to its students to develop and improve the standard of education at Vydehi. The students are trained to become individuals who will be able to convert the society by making others socially aware and liable thereby can achieve endless personal and professional rewards. Vydehi’s educational program is crafted, aiming to teach students about health development, human values, compassion, sympathy, honesty,

  • The study program of Vydehi Medical Science and Research Centre is aimed at-
  • Outstanding education and training for all of the students to prepare them for the noble profession.
  • To encourage and motivate them to achieve excellence in this field, stretching their hand to the well-being of society.
  • To develop their sensitivity and observation skills for the patient's needs which allows them to work anywhere in India and abroad.
  • To maintain a spirit of charity and cooperation to improve patient safety and promote good health in rural and underserved communities.

PLACEMENT

The high-quality medical course module and teaching methodology of Vydehi Institute of Medical Science and Research Centre, help the college in bringing successful and rewarding placement opportunities to the campus. The placement cell works tirelessly to bring the best hospitals to the campus every year for placements. 80% of the students who are pursuing medical studies get placed at reputed hospitals in India. Lucrative salary packages are offered for the students getting jobs through campus placements. Some of them get opportunities to get work with the parent hospital of Vydehi Institute of Medical Sciences and Research Centre under respective departments. Students also get one and half years of internship after completing four years of study.

FACULTY

The institute is incorporated with well-trained, experienced, loyal, and devoted faculty members to impart quality and specialized education and training to the students in each department including Medicine, Nursing, Dentistry Allied Science, and many more. Most of them are holding the Doctorate.

EXAMS

All admissions are conducted based on marks obtained in NEET conducted by the Central Board of Secondary Education. For admission to PG courses, students need to appear for the All IndiaPG Entrance Test and obtain the relevant cutoff marks.

Eligibility

Course
Eligibility
Undergraduate Courses
A candidate must have obtained not less than 50 percent marks in English and 50 Percent marks in Physics, Chemistry, and Biology in the qualifying examination.
The students aspiring for admission are required to appear and qualify for the NEET(National Eligibility and Entrance Test) He/she shall complete the age of 17 years for admission to the MBBS.
Post Graduate Courses
Applicants must meet all requirements stipulated by the Medical Council of India. Accordingly, they should have successfully passed their M.B.B.S. from an institution recognized by the Medical Council of India and must have completed or completed their respective internship.

FACILITIES

Central Library:

The digital central library of VIMS has a collection of 21,960 Medical Books & 20,200 bound volumes of journals. There is also a departmental library in every 36 Departments; there are 4387 books available in these libraries. Both the central library and the Department libraries are digital libraries with internet facilities.

The library focuses-

  • To support the research, learning, teaching, and administrative activities.
  • To provide an appropriate and comfortable environment and facilities for the individual and group study.

Digital Museum:

The Medical Museum in VIMS is the most comprehensive medical museum in India, with carefully curated specimens, information, and visuals, in its various departments - Anatomy, Pathology, Pharmacology, Microbiology, Radiology, Forensics, and Community Medicine, with 3432 specimens and samples on display.

Transportation Facilities:

The Institute provides transport services regularly between campus and the city. Institute buses are available to the students and faculty members for commuting from various points in the city to the Institute.

Student Mentoring:

Internal Complaints Committee

There is zero tolerance towards sexual harassment on campus. Nevertheless, if there is any, the students are free to contact the members of the Internal complaints committee.

Parent-teacher meeting

To discuss the academic progress and students’ performance, the parent-teacher meeting is convened periodically. Then special coaching classes are arranged to improve them.

Anti-ragging committee

There is zero tolerance towards ragging on the campus nevertheless, for any issues related to ragging, the students are free to contact the members of the Anti-ragging committee.

Sports and Cultural activities:

Students are encouraged to participate in indoor & outdoor sports activities, co-curricular & extra-curricular activities.

EVENTS & CO-CURRICULARS

Industrial visits

Students are taken in and out of Bengaluru for industrial visits to institutes and firms like Suvarnamukhi, Monsanto, Biocon, United Breweries, etc., where they are exposed to the industrial level of production of various products and acquainted with the (GLPs) and (OMPs) followed.

Symposium

The students are encouraged to attend and participate in state-level and national-level symposiums. They are also encouraged to conduct seminars in the college.

Placement Assistance

Various laboratories across Bengaluru conduct projects and also conduct training for the students which will help them to achieve a good job. Our students have been placed in companies such as Monsanto, Panacea Biotech, Biocon, Genotypic, Quintiles.

Career guidance

Eminent scientists from various BT industries give career talks to bridge the gap between the industry and college level. They guide the students on job openings for various fields in biotechnology.

FAQ

How to join the Vydehi Institute of Medical Science and Research Centre What are the admission criteria for MBBS?

Here admission will be provided based on rank obtained in the NEET examination conducted by the Central Board of Secondary Education. Seats are allocated on a merit basis through counseling conducted by the Karnataka Examination Authority.

What is the admission procedure for foreign students?

The candidate must have passed the 10+2 examination, with 50% aggregate marks in Physics, Chemistry, and Biology, and had English as one of the subjects. The candidate must have documents to prove NRI/FOREIGN status.

What are the career opportunities after MBBS?

The students after completion of their MBBS degree can practice as general practitioners provided they are registered under a State government. They can work as private practitioners as well.
