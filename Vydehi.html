OVER VIEW

Established in the year 2000, the Vydehi Institute of Medical Sciences and Research Centre is situated in Bangalore. It is spread in a lush green pleasant and peaceful atmosphere. It is affiliated with Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences and approved by the Dental Council of India, and Medical Council of India. It is recognized as one of the leading medical institutes progressive to provide health care services and education with global excellence. Having highly qualified, experienced, and dedicated faculty, the institutes prioritize hands-on learning, with the best quality, comprehensive, and integrated medical education using modern technology and research. The institute also has a world-class integrated healthcare system, with a focus on sensitive patient care to underprivileged people. Its mission is to bring multinational healthcare standards within the reach of everyone.

PLACEMENT

The high-quality medical course module and teaching methodology of Vydehi Institute of Medical Science and Research Centre, help the college in bringing successful and rewarding placement opportunities to the campus. The placement cell works tirelessly to bring the best hospitals to the campus every year for placements. 80% of the students who are pursuing medical studies get placed at reputed hospitals in India. Lucrative salary packages are offered for the students getting jobs through campus placements. Some of them get opportunities to get work with the parent hospital of Vydehi Institute of Medical Sciences and Research Centre under respective departments. Students also get one and half years of internship after completing four years of study.

FACULTY

The College has very experienced and highly qualified faculties to impart quality education to the students. All the faculties are Post Graduates in their respective professions, and more than half of them hold Doctorate degrees.

EXAMS

All admissions are conducted based on marks obtained in NEET conducted by the Central Board of Secondary Education. For admission to PG courses, students need to appear for the All IndiaPG Entrance Test and obtain the relevant cutoff marks.

FAQ

What is the fee for MBBS in India?

The MBBS course fee may vary from government, private and deemed universities. The average MBBS fee for government medical colleges in India ranges from INR 20,000 to INR 7.5 lakh, whereas the MBBS fee for private colleges can range from INR 20 lakh to more than INR 1 Cr.

What happens when we fail in NEET?

If you fail NEET, then you will not get admission into good government medical colleges. Also, as per the new rule implemented by the CBSE, you can only give this exam thrice. So, make sure you don't lose your three precious chances.

Can I join MBBS without NEET?

Though it is not possible to get admission in MBBS without NEET in India. NEET UG exam is mandatory for admission in MBBS in India or abroad. But there are various other medical courses which you can join on the basis of class 12th marks.

Why is MBBS so Costly?

MBBS education is expensive in India mainly due to the factor that there is more demand than supply of the number of medical seats.

How is life after MBBS?

Well life after MBBS is neither very easy and nor very hard also.There are many points.

How can I do MBBS after the 12th?

The students, who have cleared the 10+2 examination and they had the Physics, Chemistry and Biology in 12th stream, may go for the MBBS degree course. The MBBS is the only course in India to become a medical doctor.
