OVER VIEW

Established by Shri Dharmasthala Manjunatheshwara Educational trust in 2016, Jain AGM ayurvedic medical college is one of the finest colleges in the city which is governed by the central council of Indian medicine and is affiliated with Rajiv Gandhi University of health and science. S.D.M Jainmatt trust is a non-profit educational organisation dedicated to promoting education and the welfare of society. Besides providing education to young aspirants wanting to make a career in this field, the college also caters to the needs of poor people and provides them with quality health care.
Ayurveda is the historical root of the Indian medicine system and the college works towards keeping that system alive to date. Considered as the mother of healing and the father of all other medical systems, beat allopathic or homeopathic; Ayurveda believes in treating through nature and holistic approach and this is exactly what college believes in and provides education to students in this particular field called as “AYURVEDA”. Adding to this, the students at the college not only learn about Ayurveda and medical skills but also about the humanitarian touch and learn through exploring.
Jain AGM medical college is a private unaided college spread over an area of 5 acres in which the college constitutes around 4900 square metres and the rest of the area is occupied by the hospital and the hostels. It provides an undergraduate degree to students who want to explore the science of nature through Ayurveda.

Education at East Point Medical College?

Coming to the education at Jain AGM medical college, the institute provides a very good quality of education to the students. It believes in learning through nurturing and thereby gives a holistic approach to the students that provides support to them as a whole and not just focussing on one aspect. This helps them to stimulate innovation by inspiring new ideas and creative thinking. The teachers at the college are exceptional with more than a decade of experience in their field which not only teaches students what is written inside the books but also gives them an insight into what life is all about and how the professional sector functions since practical knowledge is also as important as theoretical knowledge in the medical profession.
Ayurveda is all about living a long and healthy life without any difficulty and much suffering since it involves the use of nature and its elements to cure the disease and eliminate its root cause and the institute is navigated towards giving this heritage of our ancestors to the new and upcoming generation of the country that will help the mankind to live a brighter and cheerful life.

MISSION & VISION

Mission statement: The college is dedicated to propagating the roots of the historical medical system of India called Ayurveda which is one of the oldest medicine systems to date that was forgotten by the people for many years. Adding to this the college mission is to impart quality education to its students and prepare them as ayurvedic physicians who are not only capable but also responsible both towards their career as well as society.

Vision statement: The college's vision is to spread the knowledge of Ayurveda to the younger generation of the country which was given to us by our ancestors as a part of our culture and heritage. The vision embodies the highest values and inspiration to the students so that they could rise above their weaknesses and look for their strength to be good human beings.

Faculty

The term Ayurveda is made up of two Sanskrit words, AYUR which means life, and VEDA that means science or knowledge, and the teaching faculty at the college not only teaches this “science of life” to its students but also focuses on bringing harmony and balance in all areas of life including mind, body and the spirit. All the educators are well experienced having more than a decade of experience in teaching. Since students of the college and the youth are the future of the country, the faculty left no stone unturned in making sure that every graduate of their college is not only a professional doctor but also a kind human being having a sense of responsibility and sympathy towards mankind especially towards the weaker section of the society.

    COURSES OFFERED

    BAMS (Bachelors in Ayurveda, medicine, and surgery)

    The total intake capacity of Jain AGM Medical college is 60 and the seats are filled under 3 categories

  • 9 seats by AYUSH Admissions Central Counselling Committee (15% All India Quota)
  • 15 seats by Karnataka Examinations Authority under AYUSH Counselling (State Quota)
  • 36 seats under Management Quota Category

    • ADMISSION

    Course Eligibility Criteria Admission Process
    BAMS (Bachealors in Ayurvedic, Medical & Surgery) 10+2 with physics, chemistry, biology, and English as main subjects with at least 50% scores By appearing in the NEET examination.

    PROCEDURE

    The candidates seeking an undergraduate degree in BAMS at Jain AGM ayurvedic medical college can apply either for merit or management quota.

    The details are as follows::

  • 15% all-India merit quota

    • The candidates who have qualified for their NEET examination can apply for quota admission counseling which is conducted by none other than the AYUSH admission central counseling committee, ministry of AYUSH.

  • KEA counseling (Karnataka domicile only)

    • Karnataka examination authority(KEA) conducts counseling for the state domicile students for admission to 25% of the total seats in Jain AGM Ayurveda medical college. Management quota direct admission

  • Direct admission to college under management quota is based upon a first-come and first-serve basis.

    • For further information and assistance, students are requested to contact the admission helpline of the college.

    CAMPUS

    COLLEGE LIBRARY

    The college has a central library which is located in the middle of the college for easy access by both the students and the staff members. The library has a huge collection of not only academic books related to Ayurveda but also has several journals, magazines, periodicals, newspapers for the benefit of the students. Adding to this the college library is also well equipped with books of general reading and overall personality development.

    INFRASTRUCTURE AND FACILITIES

    HOSTELS

    The college has well-furnished hostels for both the boys and girls with accommodation of around 100 to 150 students in each hostel. It provides them with all the basic facilities and the accommodation fee is around 5000 per month.

    AYURVEDIC HOSPITAL

    The college has an ayurvedic hospital that caters to the needs of the students and provides them with the facility needed for clinical and practical training. Not only this but also the hospital looks after the patients with utmost care and patience.

    HERBAL GARDEN

    The institute has a various types of medicinal flowers and herbs that are required by the students during their practical training as ayurvedic physicians. The herbal garden has a variety of medicinal plants that are used for day to day practical classes.

    FAQ

    How do I apply to Jain AGM ayurvedic medical college?

    Students seeking admission in Jain AGM medical college has to qualify the basic requirement of clearing their neet examination conducted by NTA and then need to apply for admission either through merit or management quota.

    How much is the fee for BAMS?

    The fee for BAMS is around INR 200500 per year.

    Is Jain AGM a private college or the government?

    Jain AGM ayurvedic medical college is a private un-aided college affiliated with Rajiv Gandhi institute of health and science and is governed by the central council of Indian medicine.

    Do they have hostel facilities at Jain AGM?

    Yes, the college provides hostels fasciitis to its students for both boys and girls. It has separate hostels with an accommodation capacity of around 150 students. The hostel fee is around 5000 per month.
