OVERVIEW

One of the most popular medical colleges in India. Recognised by National Assessment and Accreditation Council (NAAC) also approved by Medical Council of India (MCI). St. John’s medical college was established in 1963 with the aim of training healthcare professionals. St John’s medical college offers Undergraduate, Postgraduate, elective, super specialty, PHD and fellowship courses. With more than 40 departments that includes General Surgery, Medical Oncology, Cardiology, Biochemistry, etc and many of these courses are recognised and approved by Medical Council of India (MCI) A completely self funded institute that attracts faculty and students from all over the world.

How is Education at St. John’s Medical College?

One of the best medical colleges in the nation with a state of the art hospital that has 1350 beds for medical and surgical departments, also includes the facilities of super specialties departments. A college of nursing with top class facilities and a first ever research institute in a medical school. The College boasts of its 132 acre campus, which takes advantage of the salutary Bangalore weather. In December 1994, St John’s National Academy of Health Sciences was named with five units under their umbrella:

  • St. John’s Medical College.
  • St. John’s Medical College Hospital
  • St. John’s College of Nursing
  • St. John’s Research Institute
  • St. John’s Institute of Health Management and Paramedical Studies.

FAQ

Is St John's Medical College good?

With State of the art facilities and high value education, St John’s Medical college is one of the best colleges to study in Bangalore. This college is ranked as the 4th Best Medical College consecutively since 2014 and as one of the Top 25 Medical College’s in India by The Outlook India Magazine.

What is the fee in St John's Medical College?

The fees for BSc is Rs 83,550 (annual), Fees charged for BPT is Rs 92,450, For MBBS, the fees is Rs 6.28 lakh (annual), For MHA in St John’s National Academy of Health Sciences are Rs 97,550, The fees for MSc is Rs 1.12 lakh, For MD is from the range Rs 1.48 lakh to Rs 9.70 lakh.The fees for DM is around Rs 9.1 lakh.

Is there a management quota in St John's Medical College?

Yes, the management quota seat is based on cut offs given by Counselling authority. A student has to score a minimum of 137 marks or 50 percentile and in General category in order to be eligible for the same.

How can I get admission in St John's medical College for MBBS?

The candidate must have scored a minimum of 50% aggregate marks in Physics, Chemistry, Biology/ Biotechnology with English also as one of the main subjects in order to get admissions in St John’s Medical College for MBBS.

What is the scope after MBBS?

After completing MBBS, a student can pursue Doctor of Medicine (MD), Master of Surgery (MS) or Diploma. You can also pursue MBA in Healthcare Management, Hospital Administration, Hospital Management, Bioinformatics and Medical Administration to have a better knowledge on how to work with a team and get the best of the services. You can also be dedicated towards Clinical research or continue to pursue Masters in other fields of medicine like MHA (Masters in Health Administration), MSc, CMS (Combined Medical Sciences) or even Masters in Public Health. The scope after MBBS is unlimited.
