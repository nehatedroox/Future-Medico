OVER VIEW

Named after its founder, Late Justice K S Hegde who had served as the former judge of the supreme court of India and a speaker of Lok sabha, KS Hegde medical academy was established in 1999 which afterward became a constituent college of NITTE (Deemed to be University) in 2009. Although it has been only 22 years since the institute came into existence yet it has paved its way in becoming a medical institute with a high academic standard that aims at providing quality education to the children so that they can excel in their life not only as a single human being catering to their individual need but also as professional doctors who look after the need of others. KS Hegde medical academy strives to generate future doctors for the country who are not only competent but also empowered to face challenges with ethical integrity and strong moral values.The college has been recognized by the Medical Council of India and the Malaysian medical council. The institute offers a variety of courses to its students under its UG and PG curriculum. The college offers MBBS, MS/MD, and MCh programs recognized by the medical council of India.Besides this it offers a B.Sc course in Medical Imaging Technology, Medical Laboratory Technology, Anaesthesia & Operation Theatre Technology, Respiratory Therapy, Master's program in Public Health, Medical Imaging Technology, Medical Laboratory Technology, Anaesthesia & Operation Theatre Technology, Respiratory Therapy, and Hospital Administration.

HIGHLIGHTS: HOW IS EDUCATION AT KS HEGDE?

Talking about the education at KS Hegde, the institute not only provides quality education with a working environment to its students for their academic needs but also ensures to enhance and develop their character as an individual by generating competent skilled manpower so that they can face the scientific and social challenges in their life. The college provides various opportunities for student research activity which helps them in learning through discovery. Adding to this, the college has established successful research collaborations with various institutions around the globe like Catholic University Of Korea, Seoul; Christ Church University, UK; Wake Forest University, USA, and many more. A variety of cultural programs are also organized by the college to embark a sense of creativity in children. For better exposure to clinical sides, students are taken to frequent hospital visits which give them an insight into how the professional world functions, thus motivating them to excel not only theoretically by cramming their textbooks but also practically as a professional doctor.

PLACEMENT

The high-quality medical course module and teaching methodology of Vydehi Institute of Medical Science and Research Centre, help the college in bringing successful and rewarding placement opportunities to the campus. The placement cell works tirelessly to bring the best hospitals to the campus every year for placements. 80% of the students who are pursuing medical studies get placed at reputed hospitals in India. Lucrative salary packages are offered for the students getting jobs through campus placements. Some of them get opportunities to get work with the parent hospital of Vydehi Institute of Medical Sciences and Research Centre under respective departments. Students also get one and half years of internship after completing four years of study.

FACULTY

The various departments at KSHEMA are managed by several experienced and competent doctors who are dedicated to providing not only quality education to children but also working towards giving them a sense of responsibility required to be a doctor.

EXAMS

All admissions are conducted based on marks obtained in NEET conducted by the Central Board of Secondary Education. For admission to PG courses, students need to appear for the All IndiaPG Entrance Test and obtain the relevant cutoff marks.

Eligibility

Course
Eligibility
MBBS
The candidate must have completed 10+2 with at least 50% marks with physics, chemistry, biology/biotechnology and English as main subjects.The candidate should qualify for the entrance examination of NEET(UG) conducted by NTA The candidate must be of 17 years of age as of 31 Dec at the time of admission In the case of NRI or foreign students, they need to give some other documents like student visa, residential permit, AIDS-free certificate.
MD/MS
The candidate should have an MBBS degree from a recognized medical college with a permanent registration by the Medical Council Of India He should qualify for the basic entrance level called NEET(PG) conducted by NTA Admission to both NRI and general category seats in MD/MS is based upon the merit system

SPECIALIZATION
ADMISSION PROCEDURE
MBBS
In addition to the eligibility criteria mentioned above, the candidate needs to qualify for the NEET examination since the whole admission procedure is based upon the marks or the rank secured by him/her. The application form can be submitted online through the official website

FACILITIES

College Library:

KS Hegde medical academy has a fully furnished central library with a floor area of 2512 square meters. It is fully air-conditioned and equipped with fire alarms and CCTV surveillance. The college has around 18000 books and can accommodate 300 to 350 people at one go. The library is enriched with various types of printed books, journals, e-books. Its collection also includes 68 foreign books and 74 Indian books at present.

Hostels:

The hostels for the students are close to the college and the hospitals so that students can travel to and forth easily without any hassle. FAQ

How do I apply to KS Hegde Medical Academy?

The process for application is quite simple and all you need to do is to give the entrance exam for admission in a UG or PG course and be eligible for admission. For further information please visit the college website

How much are the fees for MBBS?

The fees for MBBS of KS Hegde medical academy range from around 1500,000 to 16,00,000

What are the career opportunities after MBBS?

The student after obtaining his or her graduate degree in medical can either work as a private practitioner or can choose to work as a general practitioner provided he is registered with the Medical Council of India and under any state government.

Do they have hostel facilities at college?

The college provides its students with hostel facilities separately for both boys and girls. The hostels are properly maintained with facilities like wifi connection, solar operated water heater, 24 hours surveillance, and many more

What are the career opportunities after MBBS?

The students after completion of their MBBS degree can practice as general practitioners provided they are registered under a State government. The students can work as junior doctors in medical colleges and after passing out they can work as medical consultants, doctors with various hospitals, and private clinics. They can work as private practitioners as well.

Do they have hostel facilities at the Institute of Medical Science and Research Centre?

Yes. There are separate accommodations for boys and girls, with a staying capacity of 2139 students in total.

What is the admission procedure for foreign students?

The admission procedure for foreign students is the same as that of any other student, but the only thing is that he/she needs to submit some other documents like a student visa, residential permit for the period they will stay in the country, AIDS-free certificate and no objection certificate.
