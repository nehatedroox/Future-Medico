OVER VIEW

Established in 1984-85 JSS Medical College, Mysuru, has been imparting education in medical sciences (MBBS) courses and other Undergraduate and Postgraduate education in medical sciences for the last four decades. It was established as a constituent college of JSS Academy of Higher Education & Research ( Formerly known as JSS University ) under Section-3 of UGC Act from 28-05-2008 and has been accredited A+ grade by NAAC consistently since its foundation,
JSS Medical College is located at Sri Shivarathreeshwara Nagara, Mysuru, Karnataka, India on a lush green campus of 43 acres. The college was affiliated to the University of Mysore from 1984 to 1995 and later to the Rajiv Gandhi University of Health Sciences, Mysore till 2008. The college aims at imparting excellent quality medical education which in turn would help to improve the health sector and cater to the needs of people from all strata of the society.

The institution's administration and teachers have always strived to provide high-quality education to students from all walks of life from across the country. Alumni of the college hold top positions in the healthcare and pharmaceutical industries. The program is planned and crafted to assist students in learning and comprehending numerous aspects of becoming the finest pharmaceutical practitioner possible. The students are taught by a group of experienced lecturers and professors who cater to learning possibilities in professional services as well as community healthcare.

The pupils are taught to become individuals who can change society by making others more socially aware and responsible. PLACEMENT

Since this is a medical college there is no provision for placing the students in the formal sense and the college does not have placement cell-like colleges of other disciplines. The students after completion of their MBBS degree can practice as general practitioners provided they are registered under a State government. The students can work as junior doctors in medical colleges and after passing out they can work as medical consultants, doctors with various hospitals, and private clinics. They can also work as private practitioners as well. The student’s diploma courses will have employment opportunities in Hospitals, Nursing Homes, Rehabilitation Centers, private clinics, as well as freelancing. There are various spas, gymnasiums, health centers coming up in big cities and towns run by prominent hotel chains which provide ample job opportunities for professionals trained in physiotherapy. In this field, professionals can earn anywhere between 4lakhs to 12 lakhs per year depending upon the location and organization. The average nurse, physiotherapist, or medical equipment handler is expected to earn around 6lakhs per annum. After sufficient years of experience, the yearly earnings of an individual can go as high as 12-14 lakhs per year.

FACULTY

To provide quality education to the students, the college has a very experienced and well-skilled faculty. All of the professors are Post Graduates in their fields, with more than half of them holding Doctorate degrees. There are also well-qualified staff nurses and non-teaching staff members to allocate the students in their learning process.

EXAMS

All admissions are conducted on the basis of marks obtained in NEET conducted by the Central Board of Secondary Education. For admission to PG courses, the students need to appear for NEET(PG) and obtain the relevant cutoff marks.

ADMISSION

The College offers Graduation and Post – Graduation courses such as MBBS, MD, and Diploma courses in Anatomy, Physiology, Microbiology, Pediatrics, Gastronomy and many other branches. The admission is given on the basis of NEET and other All India Examinations for various courses like BPT, BDS, Nursing and other courses. Some of the courses also require the candidate to appear for personal interview rounds. The entrance examinations are usually held around the month of May and June.

CAMPUS

The vibrant campus life encourages students to be healthier, more focused, and to have access to all current educational amenities. At the same time, the institution has the appropriate limits and guidelines in place to protect the safety and discipline of all students, which are strictly implemented by the hostel officials. Students can also stay in well-kept and reasonably priced hostels at the college.The college sports facilities for playing the games which include both outdoor and indoor. FACILITIES

HOSTEL-

Boys and females have their own hostels at the college. Each student has their own study area, as well as common spaces and linked restrooms in the dormitories. Both hostels provide well-furnished, high-class amenities to provide a home away from home experience.

STUDENT EXCHANGE PROGRAM-

To help the student's desire to cater to global communities after they have become professionals, the College provides opportunities to undergraduate students to visit foreign universities with which they have a Memorandum of Understanding signed. This helps the students to understand and experience global standards of medical practice and understand different cultures and practices. As a part of the Student Exchange Program, foreign students also visit the College and foreign professors give periodic visits to the College as guest lecturers

CENTRAL LIBRARY–

The college features a well-maintained and exclusive central library that has a multi-level collection of 44931 books, journals, and research papers that cover immense literature for all medical branches offered by the college. The library is well organized with reference textbooks, reference journals, dictionaries, past projects, and project reports, data books, etc. Among some of the notable facilities included in the library are;
1.References
2.Circulation of documents
3.Online and offline databases including Online directories and CD-ROMs
4.A cloud campus with high speed internet via wifi
5.Exclusive access to top online databases and information banks such as ClinicalKey, Scopus, AccessMedicine, AccessSurgery, Knowledge Genie-Abstract Database, etc.
As a member of the HELINET Consortium, the library provides students from all over the world with access to high-quality medical periodicals and research papers.

INFRASTRUCTURE

ADVANCED LEARNING CENTER:

The Institute has state-of-the-art air-conditioned lecture theatres which are designed to accommodate 250 students at a time. The lecture halls are equipped with the latest audio-visual technology which includes LCD projectors, microphones, projectors, slides, etc. The individual departments have seminar rooms used for the purpose of seminars and practical demonstrations with a seating capacity of 25 to 40 with audio-visual aids.
There are also digital cameras installed to record live lectures, which are converted into digital format and uploaded in the Learning Management System to facilitate E-learning. Multi-purpose halls are used for conducting internal and university exams which are equipped with a surveillance system to monitor the students during the examination.

OPERATION LABS:

There are two types of labs in the College.

  1. Medical Skills Lab- Here the students are taught the practical basics of surgery, life support, advanced cardiac life support, and other skills at post-graduate, graduate, and nursing levels using mannequins, models, and simulation techniques.
  2. Cadaveric Research and Training Unit– Here the students get to opportunity to have specific training to hone their practical skills
